## The Golden Rules

> **On a standard die, opposite faces always sum to 7.**
> Pairs: (1, 6), (2, 5), (3, 4)

> **When reading dice views, adjacent faces can NEVER be opposite.**

## Key Concepts at a Glance

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Opposite faces | Two faces that do not share any edge |
| Adjacent faces | Two faces that share an edge |
| Standard die | 1 opposite 6, 2 opposite 5, 3 opposite 4 |
| Non-standard die | Face arrangement is given via views — do NOT assume sum = 7 |
