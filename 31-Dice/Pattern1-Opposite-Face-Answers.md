# Opposite Face — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** On a standard die, what face is opposite to 2?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Standard die
- Find: Face opposite to 2

**💡 What I'll use:** Sum-to-7 rule

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Opposite of 2 = 7 − 2 = **5**

**✅ Answer: 5**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** Which pair is a valid opposite pair on a standard die?
(A) 1 and 4  (B) 2 and 5  (C) 3 and 6  (D) 1 and 3

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Standard die; opposite pairs sum to 7
- Find: Which option is a correct opposite pair

**💡 What I'll use:** Sum-to-7 rule

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Check sums
- (A) 1+4 = 5 ≠ 7 ✗
- (B) 2+5 = 7 ✓
- (C) 3+6 = 9 ≠ 7 ✗
- (D) 1+3 = 4 ≠ 7 ✗

**✅ Answer: (B) 2 and 5**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** The sum of three pairs of opposite faces of a die is:
(A) 18  (B) 21  (C) 14  (D) 7

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Standard die
- Find: Sum of ALL three opposite-face pair sums

**💡 What I'll use:** Each pair sums to 7; there are 3 pairs

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Each pair: (1,6)→7, (2,5)→7, (3,4)→7

**✅ Answer: (B) 21**
