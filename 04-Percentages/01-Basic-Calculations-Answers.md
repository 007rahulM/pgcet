# Percentages: Basic Calculations — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** What is 40% of 250?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Percentage = 40%, Whole = 250
- Find: The value that is 40% of 250

**💡 What I'll use:** Part = (Percentage / 100) × Whole

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply the formula
= (40 / 100) × 250 = 0.40 × 250 = **100**

**✅ Answer: 100**

---

### Q2

**❓ Question:** 90 is what % of 360?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Part = 90, Whole = 360
- Find: What percentage 90 is of 360

**💡 What I'll use:** Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Substitute into the formula
= (90 / 360) × 100 = (1/4) × 100 = **25%**

**✅ Answer: 25%**

---

### Q3

**❓ Question:** A score of 75 out of 150. What %?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Score = 75, Total = 150
- Find: Percentage score

**💡 What I'll use:** Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply the formula
= (75 / 150) × 100 = 0.5 × 100 = **50%**

**✅ Answer: 50%**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** If 60% of a number is 48, find the number.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 60% of some unknown number = 48
- Find: The original number

**💡 What I'll use:** Whole = (Part × 100) / Percentage

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Rearrange and solve
Number = 48 / 0.60 = (48 × 100) / 60 = **80**

**✅ Answer: 80**

---

### Q5

**❓ Question:** A earns ₹600, B earns ₹500. A's salary is what % more than B's?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A's salary = ₹600, B's salary = ₹500
- Find: By what % A earns more than B (B is the base for comparison)

**💡 What I'll use:** % more = (Difference / B's salary) × 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Calculate the difference
Difference = 600 − 500 = ₹100

Step 2: Express as % of B's salary (the base)
% more = (100 / 500) × 100 = **20%**

**✅ Answer: A earns 20% more than B**
