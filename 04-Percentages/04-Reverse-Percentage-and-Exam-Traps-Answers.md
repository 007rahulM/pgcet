# Reverse Percentage & Exam Traps — Practice Problem Solutions

## 1) A shirt marked ₹500 is sold at 10% discount. What extra discount is needed to make SP ₹405?
- After 10% discount: 500 × 90/100 = 450
- Extra reduction needed = 45
- Extra discount = 45/450 × 100 = **10%**

## 2) A town's population falls from 48000 to 42000. Find the % decrease.
- Decrease = 6000
- % decrease = 6000/48000 × 100 = **12.5%**

## 3) After a 25% increase, a number becomes 150. Find the original.
- Original = 150 × 100 / 125 = **120**

## 4) After a 10% decrease, the value becomes 540. Find the original.
- Original = 540 × 100 / 90 = **600**

## 5) A price first falls 20% and then rises 10%. Find the net % change.
- Net = −20 + 10 + (−20×10)/100 = −10 − 2 = **−12%**
