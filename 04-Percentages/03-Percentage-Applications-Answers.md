# Percentages: Applications — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** A's income is 25% more than B's. B's income is what % less than A's?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A = B + 25% of B → A = 1.25B
- Find: How much % less B is compared to A (A is now the base)

**💡 What I'll use:** % less = (Difference / A) × 100 = x / (100 + x) × 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Let B = 100, then A = 125

Step 2: Find % by which B is less than A (A is the base)
% less = (125 − 100) / 125 × 100 = 25 / 125 × 100 = **20%**

**✅ Answer: B's income is 20% less than A's**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** In an election, candidate A gets 40% of votes and loses by 1200. Find total votes.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A gets 40% of votes; the other candidate (B) gets 60%; A loses by 1200 votes
- Find: Total number of votes

**💡 What I'll use:** Difference in votes = (60% − 40%) of total = 20% of total

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Express the margin mathematically
Difference % = 60% − 40% = 20%

Step 2: 20% of total votes = 1200
Total = 1200 / 0.20 = **6,000 votes**

**✅ Answer: 6,000 total votes**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** 30% of students in a class are girls. If there are 21 girls, how many total students?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Girls = 30% of total, number of girls = 21
- Find: Total number of students

**💡 What I'll use:** Total = Part / Percentage = 21 / 0.30

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Set up the equation
30% of Total = 21

Step 2: Solve
Total = 21 / 0.30 = 21 × (100/30) = **70 students**

**✅ Answer: 70 students**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** A man spends 75% of his salary and saves ₹5000. Find his salary.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Spends 75%, so saves 25%; savings = ₹5000
- Find: Total salary

**💡 What I'll use:** 25% of salary = ₹5000 → Salary = 5000 / 0.25

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Savings = 100% − 75% = 25% of salary

Step 2: Solve for salary
25% of Salary = 5000
Salary = 5000 / 0.25 = **₹20,000**

**✅ Answer: ₹20,000**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** A number is first multiplied by 1.2 and then divided by 0.8. Net % change?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Operation 1 → multiply by 1.2 (= +20%); Operation 2 → divide by 0.8 (= multiply by 1.25 = +25%)
- Find: Net percentage change

**💡 What I'll use:** Combined factor = 1.2 / 0.8; net % = (factor − 1) × 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Combine both operations
Net factor = 1.2 / 0.8 = 1.5

Step 2: Interpret
1.5 means the number becomes 1.5× original

Net change = (1.5 − 1) × 100 = **50% increase**

**✅ Answer: 50% increase**
