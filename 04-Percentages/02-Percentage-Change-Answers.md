# Percentages: Percentage Change — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** A population of 20,000 increases by 5%. New population?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Initial population = 20,000, increase = 5%
- Find: New population after the increase

**💡 What I'll use:** New value = Original × (100 + increase%) / 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply the increase multiplier
New population = 20,000 × (105 / 100) = 20,000 × 1.05 = **21,000**

**✅ Answer: 21,000**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** Price rises 10% then falls 10%. Net change?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: First a 10% increase, then a 10% decrease on the new value
- Find: Net % change from the original price

**💡 What I'll use:** Net % = a + b + (a × b) / 100 (use − for decrease)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply the successive change formula
a = +10, b = −10
Net = 10 + (−10) + (10 × (−10)) / 100 = 0 − 1 = **−1%**

Step 2: Verify with numbers
100 → ×1.10 = 110 → ×0.90 = 99 → Net change = −1% ✓

**✅ Answer: −1% (a net decrease of 1%)**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** Price increases by 25%, then by 20%. Net increase?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Two successive increases of 25% and 20%
- Find: Total net percentage increase

**💡 What I'll use:** Net % = a + b + (a × b) / 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply the formula
Net = 25 + 20 + (25 × 20) / 100 = 45 + 5 = **50%**

**✅ Answer: 50% net increase**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** After 2 successive 10% reductions, what is the final value if original is 1000?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Original value = 1000, two successive 10% reductions
- Find: Final value

**💡 What I'll use:** Final = Original × (1 − 0.10)² = Original × (0.9)²

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply both reductions
Final = 1000 × 0.9 × 0.9 = 1000 × 0.81 = **810**

**✅ Answer: 810**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** A product's price was first increased by 30%, then decreased by 20%. What is the net % change?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: First +30%, then −20%
- Find: Net % change overall

**💡 What I'll use:** Net % = a + b + (a × b) / 100 (use − for decrease)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Substitute a = +30, b = −20
Net = 30 + (−20) + (30 × (−20)) / 100 = 10 − 6 = **+4%**

Step 2: Verify
100 → ×1.30 = 130 → ×0.80 = 104 → Net = +4% ✓

**✅ Answer: +4% net increase**
