# Binomial Expansion — Practice Questions

1. Find the coefficient of \(x^4\) in \((1+2x)^6\).
2. In \((1+x)^n\), coefficients of 3rd and 4th terms are in ratio \(5:4\). Find \(n\).
3. Find the 5th term in expansion of \((2x-3)^7\).
4. In \((1+x)^n\), if three consecutive coefficients are in ratio \(1:7:42\), find \(n\).
5. Find the term independent of \(x\) in \((x^2+\frac{1}{x})^9\).
