# Pattern 4: Science, Tech & Health

## 🔍 How to Recognize This Pattern

- Questions on WHO, ISRO, AI, GPS, Wi-Fi
- Renewable energy or health institution questions
- Basic science/technology awareness

---

## 🧠 The Golden Rule

> **Remember technology term → what it does → which institution/body it belongs to.**

## 📌 Core Notes

- **WHO** = World Health Organization; HQ = Geneva.
- **ISRO** = India's space agency.
- **AI** = Artificial Intelligence.
- **GPS** helps location and navigation.
- **Renewable energy** includes solar, wind, hydro.
- Public health topics often ask about vaccination, sanitation, or disease prevention.

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step Mini Examples

### Example 1
**Q:** What is the headquarters of WHO?

**Thinking:** WHO → global health body → Geneva.

**Answer:** Geneva

---

### Example 2
**Q:** Name one renewable source of energy.

**Thinking:** Solar / wind / hydro are standard examples.

**Answer:** Solar energy

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Strategy

1. Identify if it is institution, technology, or health topic.
2. Recall the one-line use.
3. Give the most standard accepted answer.
