# Pattern 6 Answers: 2025 Emerging Terms

## 1) Define strike price in one line.
**Answer:** The pre-agreed price at which the underlying asset can be bought/sold in an options contract.

## 2) What does cabotage refer to?
**Answer:** Transport of goods or passengers within the same country.

## 3) Which LEARN step is about collaborative decision?
**Answer:** **Negotiate**.

## 4) Why is LiDAR used in ADAS?
**Answer:** It detects obstacles and measures distance accurately to improve vehicle safety assistance.

## 5) Name two factors that directly affect bond value.
**Answer:** Interest rates and issuer credit rating (inflation is also a major factor).

## 6) Which NCTE role is valid: norms setting or job placement?
**Answer:** **Norms setting** is valid; direct job placement is not an NCTE core function.
