# Pattern 6: 2025 Emerging Terms (Finance, Policy, Transport, Health-Tech)

## 🔍 How to Recognize This Pattern

- "Strike price", "cabotage", "NCTE", "PMSBY"
- "LEARN model", "HBM", "inclusive workplace"
- "ADAS", "LiDAR", "bond value factors"

---

## 🧠 The Golden Rule

> **For emerging terms, learn each item as: domain + one-line definition + one common exam trap.**

---

## 📌 Core Notes

- **Strike Price (Options):** price at which the underlying can be bought/sold if option is exercised.
- **Cabotage:** movement of goods/passengers within a country by transport operators.
- **NCTE:** sets norms/standards for teacher education; does **not** do direct recruitment or guarantee jobs.
- **PMSBY:** accidental insurance cover generally cited as **₹2 lakh**.
- **HBM (Health Belief Model):** behavior model based on perceived risk, severity, benefits, barriers.
- **LEARN model (health communication):** Listen, Explain, Acknowledge, Recommend, Negotiate.
- **ADAS:** vehicle safety assistance technologies.
- **LiDAR:** sensor technology used in ADAS for obstacle detection and distance measurement.
- **Bond value drivers:** interest rates, inflation, issuer credit quality.
- **Inclusive workplace drivers:** diversity training, equal-opportunity policy, inclusive leadership, flexibility.

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step Mini Examples

### Example 1
**Q:** In options trading, what is strike price?

**Thinking:** Option contract term -> exercise price, not market price.

**Answer:** Exercise price of underlying asset.

---

### Example 2
**Q:** LEARN model step focused on jointly deciding treatment?

**Thinking:** "Jointly decide" means final agreement stage.

**Answer:** Negotiate.

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Strategy

1. Identify the domain first (finance/transport/health/governance/tech).
2. Recall one-line definition.
3. Eliminate options using common traps (market price vs strike price, LiDAR vs RFID, etc.).
