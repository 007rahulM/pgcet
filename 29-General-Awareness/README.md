# General Awareness — Overview

This folder now follows the same structured style as the other strong folders: **recognition clues, topic mapping, quick teaching notes, practice questions, and separate answer files**.

General Awareness in PGCET is not about memorizing everything. It is about covering the syllabus buckets clearly and revising them in a repeatable way.

## Golden Rules

- Build category-wise memory, not random memory.
- Revise full forms, institutions, and one-line definitions daily.
- For current-affairs-type questions, connect the term to its domain first.
- Practice through short repeated recall, not long passive reading.

## Study Order

2. Pattern 2 and 3 for the most exam-like one-liners.
3. Pattern 4 and 5 for mixed static + modern awareness.
