# Pattern 3: Governance & Polity

## 🔍 How to Recognize This Pattern

- Questions on President / PM / Parliament
- Constitutional or statutory bodies
- Governance institutions and their roles

---

## 🧠 The Golden Rule

> **Remember office → role → institution.**

## 📌 Core Notes

- **President** = constitutional head of India.
- **Prime Minister** = head of government.
- **Parliament** = law-making body.
- **Election Commission of India (ECI)** conducts elections.
- **CAG** audits public spending.
- **NITI Aayog** replaced the Planning Commission.

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step Mini Examples

### Example 1
**Q:** Which body conducts elections in India?

**Thinking:** Elections → Election Commission.

**Answer:** Election Commission of India (ECI)

---

### Example 2
**Q:** Which body replaced Planning Commission?

**Thinking:** Old planning body replaced by policy think tank.

**Answer:** NITI Aayog

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Strategy

1. Ask: is this office, institution, or constitutional concept?
2. Recall the key role in one line.
3. Give the exact full form where needed.
