# Governance & Polity — Pattern 3

## 🔍 When This Comes Up
- President/PM/Parliament roles
- Constitutional bodies (ECI, CAG)
- Basic governance terms

---

## 🧠 Core Idea
Remember **key offices** and **their functions**.

---

## 📌 Quick Facts
- **President** = constitutional head
- **Prime Minister** = head of government
- **ECI** conducts elections
- **CAG** audits public spending
- **NITI Aayog** replaced Planning Commission

---

## ✅ Micro Example
**Q:** Which body conducts elections in India?
**A:** Election Commission of India (ECI)

---

## 📝 Practice
1. Full form of CAG
2. Which body replaced Planning Commission?

**Answers:**
1) Comptroller and Auditor General
2) NITI Aayog
