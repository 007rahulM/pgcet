# Pattern 5: Culture, Entrepreneurship & Communication

## 🔍 How to Recognize This Pattern

- Classical dance / heritage / cultural identity questions
- Startup vocabulary questions
- Business communication basics like agenda, memo, minutes

---

## 🧠 The Golden Rule

> **Treat this pattern as three mini-topics: culture, startup terms, and communication terms.**

## 📌 Core Notes

### Culture
- Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Odissi, Kuchipudi are common examples.
- Festivals, languages, and heritage questions are usually direct recall.

### Entrepreneurship
- **Startup** = new business venture.
- **Incubator** = support system for early-stage startups.
- **Angel investor** = early individual investor.
- **VC** = venture capital funding.
- **MSME** = Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.

### Business Communication
- **Agenda** = list of items to be discussed.
- **Minutes** = written record of what was discussed/decided.
- **Memo** = short internal communication note.
- Formal email etiquette matters.

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step Mini Examples

### Example 1
**Q:** What are minutes of a meeting?

**Thinking:** Meeting record → written summary of decisions.

**Answer:** Written record of meeting discussion and decisions

---

### Example 2
**Q:** What is an incubator?

**Thinking:** Startup support structure for early-stage ventures.

**Answer:** A support system for early-stage startups

## ⚡ 60-Second Strategy

1. Identify which of the three mini-topics the question belongs to.
2. Recall the simplest exact meaning.
3. Use full forms where required.
