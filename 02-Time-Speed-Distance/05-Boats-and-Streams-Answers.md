### Q1

**❓ Question:** A boat's speed in still water is 15 km/h and the stream speed is 3 km/h. Find downstream and upstream speeds.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Boat speed (still water) = 15 km/h, Stream speed = 3 km/h
- Find: Downstream speed and Upstream speed

**💡 What I'll use:** Downstream = Boat + Stream; Upstream = Boat − Stream

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Downstream speed = 15 + 3 = **18 km/h**
Step 2: Upstream speed = 15 − 3 = **12 km/h**

**✅ Answer: Downstream = 18 km/h, Upstream = 12 km/h**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** A boat goes 24 km downstream in 3 hours and 12 km upstream in 3 hours. Find the boat's speed in still water and stream speed.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 24 km downstream in 3 hrs; 12 km upstream in 3 hrs
- Find: Boat speed in still water and stream speed

**💡 What I'll use:** Downstream speed = D/T; Upstream speed = D/T; Boat = (D+U)/2; Stream = (D−U)/2

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Downstream speed = 24 / 3 = 8 km/h
Step 2: Upstream speed = 12 / 3 = 4 km/h
Step 3: Boat speed = (8 + 4) / 2 = **6 km/h**
Step 4: Stream speed = (8 − 4) / 2 = **2 km/h**

**✅ Answer: Boat speed = 6 km/h, Stream speed = 2 km/h**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** A boat's speed is 8 km/h and stream speed is 2 km/h. How long to travel 30 km upstream?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Boat = 8 km/h, Stream = 2 km/h, Distance upstream = 30 km
- Find: Time to travel upstream

**💡 What I'll use:** Upstream speed = Boat − Stream; Time = Distance / Upstream speed

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Upstream speed = 8 − 2 = 6 km/h
Step 2: Time = 30 / 6 = **5 hours**

**✅ Answer: 5 hours**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** A boat covers 16 km downstream in 2 hours and returns 16 km upstream in 4 hours. Find the stream speed.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 16 km downstream in 2 hrs; 16 km upstream in 4 hrs
- Find: Stream speed

**💡 What I'll use:** Downstream speed = D/T; Upstream speed = D/T; Stream = (Downstream − Upstream) / 2

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Downstream speed = 16 / 2 = 8 km/h
Step 2: Upstream speed = 16 / 4 = 4 km/h
Step 3: Stream speed = (8 − 4) / 2 = **2 km/h**

**✅ Answer: 2 km/h**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** A boat travels 40 km downstream and 20 km upstream in a total of 8 hours. Downstream speed is twice upstream speed. Find both speeds.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 40 km downstream + 20 km upstream = 8 hrs; downstream speed = 2 × upstream speed
- Find: Both speeds

**💡 What I'll use:** Let upstream = x, downstream = 2x; Time equation: 40/2x + 20/x = 8

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Let upstream speed = x km/h → downstream speed = 2x km/h
Step 2: 40/(2x) + 20/x = 8 → 20/x + 20/x = 8 → 40/x = 8
Step 3: x = 40/8 = 5
Step 4: Upstream = **5 km/h**, Downstream = **10 km/h**

**✅ Answer: Upstream = 5 km/h, Downstream = 10 km/h**

### Q6

**❓ Question:** A boat's downstream speed is 18 km/h and upstream speed is 10 km/h. Find speed in still water.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Downstream = 18 km/h, Upstream = 10 km/h
- Find: Speed in still water

**💡 What I'll use:** Still water speed = (Downstream + Upstream) / 2

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Still water speed = (18 + 10) / 2 = 28 / 2 = **14 km/h**

**✅ Answer: 14 km/h**

### Q7

**❓ Question:** Stream speed is 4 km/h and a boat covers 32 km upstream in 8 hours. Find the boat's speed in still water.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Stream = 4 km/h, Distance upstream = 32 km, Time = 8 hrs
- Find: Boat speed in still water

**💡 What I'll use:** Upstream speed = Distance / Time; Still water = Upstream + Stream

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Upstream speed = 32 / 8 = 4 km/h
Step 2: Still water speed = Upstream speed + Stream speed = 4 + 4 = **8 km/h**

**✅ Answer: 8 km/h**

### Q8

**❓ Question:** A person rows 8 km with the current in 2 hours and 4 km against the current in 2 hours. Find the stream speed.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 8 km with current in 2 hrs; 4 km against current in 2 hrs
- Find: Stream speed

**💡 What I'll use:** Downstream speed = D/T; Upstream speed = D/T; Stream = (Downstream − Upstream) / 2

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Downstream speed = 8 / 2 = 4 km/h
Step 2: Upstream speed = 4 / 2 = 2 km/h
Step 3: Stream speed = (4 − 2) / 2 = **1 km/h**

**✅ Answer: 1 km/h**
