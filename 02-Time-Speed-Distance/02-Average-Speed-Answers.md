### Q1

**❓ Question:** A person goes from X to Y at 30 km/h and returns at 20 km/h. Find average speed.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Equal distances travelled at two different speeds
- Find: Average speed for the whole journey

**💡 What I'll use:** Equal-distance formula: Average Speed = 2S₁S₂ / (S₁ + S₂)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Since both legs cover the same distance, use the harmonic-mean formula.
Step 2: Average Speed = 2 × 30 × 20 / (30 + 20) = 1200 / 50 = **24 km/h**

**✅ Answer: 24 km/h**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** A car travels for 3 hours at 60 km/h and for 2 hours at 90 km/h. Find average speed.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Different times at different speeds (unequal time segments)
- Find: Average speed

**💡 What I'll use:** Average Speed = Total Distance / Total Time

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Distance in first segment = 60 × 3 = 180 km
Step 2: Distance in second segment = 90 × 2 = 180 km
Step 3: Total Distance = 180 + 180 = 360 km; Total Time = 3 + 2 = 5 hrs
Step 4: Average Speed = 360 / 5 = **72 km/h**

**✅ Answer: 72 km/h**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** A person travels from A to B at 50 km/h taking 3 hours, then returns at 30 km/h. Find average speed for the whole trip.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A→B at 50 km/h (3 hrs), B→A at 30 km/h
- Find: Average speed for the round trip

**💡 What I'll use:** Total Distance / Total Time

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Distance A to B = 50 × 3 = 150 km
Step 2: Return time = 150 / 30 = 5 hours
Step 3: Total distance = 300 km; Total time = 3 + 5 = 8 hrs
Step 4: Average Speed = 300 / 8 = **37.5 km/h**

**✅ Answer: 37.5 km/h**

### Q8

**❓ Question:** A person covers equal distances at speeds 3x and 2x. Find average speed in terms of x.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Equal distances, going speed = 3x, return speed = 2x
- Find: Average speed (algebraic expression)

**💡 What I'll use:** Equal-distance formula: 2S₁S₂ / (S₁ + S₂)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Average Speed = 2 × 3x × 2x / (3x + 2x)
Step 2: = 12x² / 5x
Step 3: = **12x / 5**

**✅ Answer: 12x/5**
