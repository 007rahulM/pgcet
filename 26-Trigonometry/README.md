## Why It Appears Every Year

From real PGCET papers:
- **2023:** sin⁻¹(sin(3π/5)), sin(x+y) given sin x and cos y values, cos(π/3) formula
- **2024:** Height of pole from shadow and angle, Trigonometric equations, Matrices with sin/cos
- **2025:** cos2x formulas, general solution of sinx = siny and tanx = tany, sin(x+y) value, angle of elevation (ladder problem)

> Simple identities, Trigonometric equations, Properties of Triangles, Inverse function.
