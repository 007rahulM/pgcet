# Pattern 6: BODMAS and Nested Expressions — Practice Problem Solutions

## 1) Evaluate `30 - [12 - {8 - (6 - 2×3)}]`
- (6 - 2×3) = 6 - 6 = 0
- {8 - 0} = 8
- [12 - 8] = 4
- 30 - 4 = **26**

## 2) Evaluate `24 ÷ 3 + 5 × (7 - 5)`
- (7 - 5) = 2
- 24 ÷ 3 = 8
- 5 × 2 = 10
- 8 + 10 = **18**

## 3) Evaluate `60 - {10 + [8 - (6 - 4)]}`
- (6 - 4) = 2
- [8 - 2] = 6
- {10 + 6} = 16
- 60 - 16 = **44**

## 4) Evaluate `48 ÷ 6 × 2 + 3`
- 48 ÷ 6 = 8
- 8 × 2 = 16
- 16 + 3 = **19**

## 5) Evaluate `25 - [10 - {6 - (8 - 3×2)}]`
- (8 - 3×2) = 8 - 6 = 2
- {6 - 2} = 4
- [10 - 4] = 6
- 25 - 6 = **19**
