# Conics & Transformations — Answers

### Q1
Compare with x^2 = 4ay:
```
4a = 16 → a = 4
```
Focus = (0, 4), Directrix = y = -4.

**Answer:** Focus (0, 4), Directrix y = -4

---

### Q2

**Answer:** Ellipse
