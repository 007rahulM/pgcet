# Pattern 3: Equation from Roots

## 🔍 How to Recognize This Pattern

- "Form the quadratic equation with roots..."
- "Given sum and product of roots"
- "Roots are α and β"
- "New roots are related to old roots"

---

## 🧠 The Golden Rule

> **If roots are α and β, the equation is built from their sum and product.**

## 📐 Core Formula

If roots are α and β:
```
x^2 - (α + β)x + αβ = 0
```

If sum = S and product = P:
```
x^2 - Sx + P = 0
```

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step Examples

### Example 1
**❓ Question:** Form the equation with roots 3 and 5.

**✏️ My Solution:**
```
Sum = 3 + 5 = 8
Product = 3 × 5 = 15
Equation: x^2 - 8x + 15 = 0
```

**✅ Answer: x^2 - 8x + 15 = 0**

---

### Example 2
**❓ Question:** Sum of roots is 7 and product is 10. Form the quadratic.

**✏️ My Solution:**
```
x^2 - 7x + 10 = 0
```

**✅ Answer: x^2 - 7x + 10 = 0**

---

### Example 3
**❓ Question:** Roots are 1 + √2 and 1 - √2. Form the equation.

**✏️ My Solution:**
```
Sum = (1 + √2) + (1 - √2) = 2
Product = (1 + √2)(1 - √2) = 1 - 2 = -1
Equation: x^2 - 2x - 1 = 0
```

**✅ Answer: x^2 - 2x - 1 = 0**

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Strategy

1. First find sum of roots.
2. Then find product of roots.
3. Put directly into x² - Sx + P = 0.
4. Simplify only at the end.
