# Equation from Roots — Pattern 3

## 🔍 When This Comes Up
- "Form the quadratic equation" given sum/product of roots
- Roots expressed in variables or parameters
- Questions about shifting or transforming roots

---

## 🧠 Core Idea
If the roots are α and β, the quadratic is built using **sum** and **product**.

---

## 📐 Key Formula
If roots are α and β:
```
x^2 - (α + β)x + (αβ) = 0
```
If sum S and product P are given:
```
x^2 - Sx + P = 0
```

---

## ✅ Worked Example

**❓ Question:** Form the equation with roots 3 and 5.

**✏️ My Solution:**
```
Sum = 3 + 5 = 8
Product = 3 × 5 = 15
Equation: x^2 - 8x + 15 = 0
```
**✅ Answer:** x^2 − 8x + 15 = 0

---

## 📝 Practice
1. Form the quadratic with roots 2 and -4
2. Roots sum to 7, product to 10. Form the equation.
3. Roots are (1 ± √5). Find the equation.

**Answers:**
1) x^2 + 2x − 8 = 0
2) x^2 − 7x + 10 = 0
3) x^2 − 2x − 4 = 0
