## 📐 Mathematics

### Time & Work
- **When to use:** "Together" / "pipes" problems
- **Formula:** Time together = (XY)/(X+Y)
- **Micro‑example:** A=10 days, B=15 days → 150/25 = **6 days**
- **Pattern:** [Pattern1-Basic-Together](../01-Time-and-Work/Pattern1-Basic-Together.md)

### Time, Speed & Distance
- **When to use:** distance/speed/time conversion
- **Formula:** D = S × T, Avg speed (equal dist) = 2ab/(a+b)
- **Micro‑example:** 60 km/h for 2 h → D = 120 km
- **Pattern:** [01-Basic-Concepts](../02-Time-Speed-Distance/01-Basic-Concepts.md)

### Profit, Loss & Discount
- **When to use:** SP/CP/discount questions
- **Formula:** Profit% = (SP−CP)/CP × 100; SP = MP × (100−D%)/100
- **Micro‑example:** CP=100, SP=120 → Profit% = 20%
- **Pattern:** [01-Basic-Concepts](../03-Profit-and-Loss/01-Basic-Concepts.md)

### Percentages
- **When to use:** successive change or % of a number
- **Formula:** x% of y = xy/100; successive change = a + b + ab/100
- **Micro‑example:** 20% of 150 = 30
- **Pattern:** [02-Percentage-Change](../04-Percentages/02-Percentage-Change.md)

### Ratio & Proportion
- **When to use:** direct ratio or partnership
- **Formula:** a:b = c:d → ad = bc
- **Micro‑example:** 2:3 = x:12 → x = 8
- **Pattern:** [01-Basic-Ratios](../05-Ratio-and-Proportion/01-Basic-Ratios.md)

### Simple & Compound Interest
- **When to use:** interest over time
- **Formula:** SI = PRT/100; CI = P[(1+R/100)^T − 1]
- **Micro‑example:** P=1000, R=10, T=1 → SI=100
- **Pattern:** [01-Simple-Interest](../06-Simple-Compound-Interest/01-Simple-Interest.md)

### Averages
- **When to use:** new member added/removed
- **Formula:** Change in avg × new count = difference
- **Micro‑example:** Avg 10 of 5 numbers. New avg 11 → sum increases by 6
- **Pattern:** [01-Basic-Average](../07-Averages/01-Basic-Average.md)

### Permutation & Combination
- **When to use:** arrangements or selections
- **Formula:** nPr = n!/(n−r)!, nCr = n!/[r!(n−r)!]
- **Micro‑example:** 5C2 = 10
- **Pattern:** [01-Permutations](../09-Permutation-Combination/01-Permutations.md)

### Probability
- **When to use:** random events
- **Formula:** P(A or B) = P(A)+P(B)−P(A∩B)
- **Micro‑example:** Two coins, exactly one head = 2/4 = 1/2
- **Pattern:** [02-Compound-Events](../10-Probability/02-Compound-Events.md)

### Sets
- **When to use:** Venn diagram counts
- **Formula:** n(A∪B) = n(A)+n(B)−n(A∩B)
- **Micro‑example:** A=20, B=15, both=5 → union=30
- **Pattern:** [Pattern1-Sets-and-Venn-Diagrams](../20-Sets-and-Relations/Pattern1-Sets-and-Venn-Diagrams.md)

### Algebra (Identities & Factorization)
- **When to use:** expand/factorize/simplify
- **Formula:** (a+b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2; a^2−b^2=(a−b)(a+b)
- **Micro‑example:** x^2+7x+12 = (x+3)(x+4)
- **Pattern:** [Pattern5-Factorization-and-Expressions](../24-Algebra-Basics/Pattern5-Factorization-and-Expressions.md)

### Quadratic Equations
- **When to use:** roots or nature of roots
- **Formula:** D = b^2 − 4ac; roots = (−b ± √D)/(2a)
- **Micro‑example:** x^2−5x+6=0 → roots 2,3
- **Pattern:** [Pattern2-Discriminant-and-Nature-of-Roots](../25-Quadratic-Equations/Pattern2-Discriminant-and-Nature-of-Roots.md)

### Coordinate Geometry
- **When to use:** distance/slope/line
- **Formula:** Distance = √[(x2−x1)^2+(y2−y1)^2]
- **Micro‑example:** (0,0) to (3,4) → 5
- **Pattern:** [Pattern2-Lines-and-Distance](../26-Coordinate-Geometry/Pattern2-Lines-and-Distance.md)

### Trigonometry
- **When to use:** evaluate or solve trig expressions
- **Formula:** sin^2θ + cos^2θ = 1; sin(A+B) = sinA cosB + cosA sinB
- **Micro‑example:** sin 30° = 1/2
- **Pattern:** [Pattern1-Trigonometry](../27-Trigonometry/Pattern1-Trigonometry.md)

### Statistics
- **When to use:** mean/variance/regression
- **Formula:** Variance = Σ(x−x̄)^2 / n
- **Micro‑example:** {2,4} → mean 3, variance = (1+1)/2 = 1
- **Pattern:** [Pattern2-Variance-and-SD](../19-Statistics/Pattern2-Variance-and-SD.md)

### Matrices
- **When to use:** determinant or inverse
- **Formula:** 2×2 det = ad − bc
- **Micro‑example:** [[1,2],[3,4]] → det = (1×4 − 2×3) = −2
- **Pattern:** [Pattern2-Determinants-and-Inverse](../21-Matrices-and-Determinants/Pattern2-Determinants-and-Inverse.md)

### Boolean Algebra
- **When to use:** simplify logic expression
- **Formula:** (A+B)' = A'·B' (De Morgan)
- **Micro‑example:** (A+B)' when A=1,B=0 → A'·B'=0
- **Pattern:** [Pattern3-Simplification-Strategies](../28-Boolean-Algebra/Pattern3-Simplification-Strategies.md)

### Counting Figures
- **When to use:** grids/blocks
- **Formula:** Rectangles in m×n = [m(m+1)/2][n(n+1)/2]
- **Micro‑example:** 2×3 grid → 18 rectangles
- **Pattern:** [Pattern6-Composite-Rectangles-and-Squares](../30-Counting-Figures/Pattern6-Composite-Rectangles-and-Squares.md)

## 💻 Computer Awareness (Quick Facts)

### Number Systems & Binary
- **When to use:** conversions or complements
- **Formula:** 1011₂ = 11₁₀; 2’s complement = invert + 1
- **Micro‑example:** 0101 → 1011 (2’s comp)

### Data Representation & Binary Arithmetic
- **When to use:** floating point or binary ops
- **Formula:** Number = Sign × Mantissa × 2^Exponent
- **Micro‑example:** 1101.01₂ = 1.10101₂ × 2^3
- **Pattern:** [09-Data-Representation-and-Binary-Arithmetic](../18-Computer-Awareness/09-Data-Representation-and-Binary-Arithmetic.md)

### Data Structures
- **When to use:** stack/queue/tree questions

- **Micro‑example:** push(5), push(3), pop → top = 5
- **Pattern:** [03-Data-Structures](../18-Computer-Awareness/03-Data-Structures.md)

### DBMS & SQL

- **Pattern:** [06-DBMS-and-SQL](../18-Computer-Awareness/06-DBMS-and-SQL.md)
