# Letter Series — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** Find the missing term: B, D, F, H, **?**

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: The series B, D, F, H
- Find: The next letter

**💡 What I'll use:** Convert to numbers, find the gap pattern, convert back

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Convert to numbers
- B=2, D=4, F=6, H=8

Step 2: Find the pattern
- 4−2=2, 6−4=2, 8−6=2 → +2 each time (skip one letter)

Step 3: Apply and convert back
- 8 + 2 = 10 = J

**✅ Answer: J**

### Q3

- Given: Paired letter series AZ, BY, CX, DW
- Find: The next pair

**💡 What I'll use:** Solve the first-letter and second-letter streams separately

**✏️ My Solution:**

- A(1), B(2), C(3), D(4) → +1 each → next = E(5)

- Z(26), Y(25), X(24), W(23) → −1 each → next = V(22)

Step 3: Combine
- E + V = EV

### Q6

- Given: Alternating series A, Z, B, Y, C, X
- Find: The next letter

**💡 What I'll use:** Two interleaved streams — one going forward, one going backward

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Separate the two streams
- Odd positions (forward): A(1), B(2), C(3) → next = D(4)
- Even positions (backward): Z(26), Y(25), X(24) → next = W(23)

Step 2: The series alternates — the 7th term is the next odd-position term
- After C (3rd forward letter), the next term is **D**

**✅ Answer: D**

### Q7

**❓ Question:** Find the missing term: A1, B4, C9, D16, **?** (squares!)

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Mixed series A1, B4, C9, D16
- Find: The next term

**💡 What I'll use:** Solve letter part and number part separately

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Letter pattern
- A(1), B(2), C(3), D(4) → +1 → next = E(5)

Step 2: Number pattern
- 1=1², 4=2², 9=3², 16=4² → perfect squares → next = 5² = 25

Step 3: Combine
- E + 25 = E25

**✅ Answer: E25**

### Q10

**❓ Question:** Find the missing term: Z, W, T, Q, **?** (skip 2 backward)

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: The series Z, W, T, Q going backward
- Find: The next letter

**💡 What I'll use:** Convert to numbers, find the gap, convert back

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Convert to numbers
- Z=26, W=23, T=20, Q=17

Step 2: Find the pattern
- 26−23=3, 23−20=3, 20−17=3 → −3 each time (skip two letters going backward)

Step 3: Apply and convert back
- 17 − 3 = 14 = N

**✅ Answer: N**
