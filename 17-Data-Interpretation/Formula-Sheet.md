## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 1 — % Change in Data

### Question looks like:
- "Production in 2020 = 500, 2021 = 600. **% increase**?"
- "Sales dropped from 800 to 640. **% decrease**?"
- "Year 1 to Year 2 growth rate?"

### ALL Formulas:

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| % Increase | `(New − Old) ÷ Old × 100` |
| % Decrease | `(Old − New) ÷ Old × 100` |
| % Change (general) | `(New − Old) ÷ Old × 100` (positive = increase, negative = decrease) |
| New value from old and %change | `Old × (100 ± %change) ÷ 100` |
| Old value from new and %change | `New × 100 ÷ (100 ± %change)` |

→ **See examples: [Pattern1-Tables-and-Bar-Graphs.md](./Pattern1-Tables-and-Bar-Graphs.md)**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 2 — Part as % of Whole

### Question looks like:
- "In 2021, company A sold 300 units out of total 1200. A's **share %**?"
- "What **% of total** is Category X?"
- "Country A contributes **how many %** to total exports?"

### ALL Formulas:

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| % share | `(Part ÷ Total) × 100` |
| Actual value from % and total | `(% ÷ 100) × Total` |
| Total from part and % | `Part × 100 ÷ %` |

→ **See examples: [Pattern1-Tables-and-Bar-Graphs.md](./Pattern1-Tables-and-Bar-Graphs.md)**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 3 — Pie Charts

### Question looks like:
- "Pie chart: Education sector = 25%. Total budget = ₹5000 crore. Education budget?"
- "Sector angle = 72°. What % of total?"
- "Convert 30% to degrees on pie chart."

### ALL Formulas:

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Degrees for a given % | `(% ÷ 100) × 360` |
| % from degrees | `(Degrees ÷ 360) × 100` |
| Value of a sector | `(% ÷ 100) × Total` or `(Degrees ÷ 360) × Total` |

→ **See examples: [Pattern2-Pie-Charts-and-Line-Graphs.md](./Pattern2-Pie-Charts-and-Line-Graphs.md)**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 4 — Average from Table / Graph

### Question looks like:
- "What is the **average** production over 5 years?"
- "Average sales of 4 companies in 2021?"

### ALL Formulas:

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Average | `Sum of all values ÷ Number of values` |
| Missing year value from average | `Average × n − known values sum` |

→ **See examples: [Pattern1-Tables-and-Bar-Graphs.md](./Pattern1-Tables-and-Bar-Graphs.md)**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 5 — Ratio and Comparison

### Question looks like:
- "**Ratio** of Company A to Company B sales in 2021?"
- "Which year had the **highest growth rate**?"
- "In which year was **value X maximum / minimum**?"

### Approach:

| Find | Approach |
|------|---------|
| Ratio A:B | `Value A ÷ Value B` (simplify) |
| Maximum growth year | Calculate % change for each period; find highest |
| Minimum value | Scan the row/column |

### Caselet (Paragraph-based DI):
1. Read carefully and **extract all numbers into a table first**
2. Then apply percentage / ratio / average formulas
3. Never skip the extraction step

→ **See examples: [Pattern3-Caselets.md](./Pattern3-Caselets.md)**
