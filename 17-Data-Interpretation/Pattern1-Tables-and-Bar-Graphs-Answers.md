### Q1

**❓ Question:** What was the % increase in Company A's revenue from 2019 to 2021?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Company A revenue — 2019 = ₹400 crore, 2021 = ₹500 crore
- Find: Percentage increase from 2019 to 2021

**💡 What I'll use:** % change = (New − Old) / Old × 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Identify values — Old = 400, New = 500

Step 2: Apply formula:
% increase = (500 − 400) / 400 × 100
= 100 / 400 × 100
= 0.25 × 100

**✅ Answer: 25%**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** What is the ratio of Company A's revenue to Company C's revenue in 2020?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 2020 row — Company A = 450, Company C = 480
- Find: Ratio A : C in simplest form

**💡 What I'll use:** Divide both values by their HCF to simplify the ratio

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Values — A = 450, C = 480

Step 2: HCF of 450 and 480
- 480 = 450 × 1 + 30
- 450 = 30 × 15 + 0 → HCF = 30

Step 3: Simplify: 450 ÷ 30 = 15, 480 ÷ 30 = 16

**✅ Answer: 15 : 16**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** What was the average annual revenue of Company B over the 4 years?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Company B — 300, 350, 320, 400 (over 4 years)
- Find: Arithmetic mean of these four values

**💡 What I'll use:** Average = Sum / Count

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Sum = 300 + 350 + 320 + 400 = 1370

Step 2: Average = 1370 / 4 = 342.5

**✅ Answer: ₹342.5 crore**

### Q6

**❓ Question:** Which company showed the highest % growth from 2019 to 2022?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Revenue of all three companies in 2019 and 2022
- Find: Which company's percentage growth (2019→2022) was the highest

**💡 What I'll use:** % growth = (2022 value − 2019 value) / 2019 value × 100 — computed for all three

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Company A — 2019 = 400, 2022 = 480
% growth = (480 − 400) / 400 × 100 = 80 / 400 × 100 = **20%**

Step 2: Company B — 2019 = 300, 2022 = 400
% growth = (400 − 300) / 300 × 100 = 100 / 300 × 100 = **33.3%**

Step 3: Company C — 2019 = 500, 2022 = 600
% growth = (600 − 500) / 500 × 100 = 100 / 500 × 100 = **20%**

Step 4: Compare — 33.3% > 20% = 20%

**✅ Answer: Company B (33.3% growth)**
