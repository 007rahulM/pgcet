## 📋 Caselet 1: College Students

**Given:** A college has 1200 students. 60% are in Science, 25% in Commerce, and the rest in Arts. 50% of Science students, 40% of Commerce students, and 30% of Arts students are girls.

**Extracted Data:**
| Stream   | % Share | Count | Girls %  | Girls | Boys |
|----------|---------|-------|----------|-------|------|
| Science  | 60%     | 720   | 50%      | 360   | 360  |
| Commerce | 25%     | 300   | 40%      | 120   | 180  |
| Arts     | 15%     | 180   | 30%      | 54    | 126  |
| **Total**|         | **1200**|        | **534**| **666**|

---

### Q1

**❓ Question:** How many students are in Arts?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Total = 1200; Science = 60%, Commerce = 25%, Arts = rest
- Find: Number of students in Arts

**💡 What I'll use:** Arts % = 100% − 60% − 25% = 15%; then Amount = (% / 100) × Total

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Arts % = 100 − 60 − 25 = 15%

Step 2: Arts students = (15 / 100) × 1200 = 180

**✅ Answer: 180 students**

---

### Q2

**❓ Question:** How many girls are in Science?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Science = 720 students; 50% are girls
- Find: Count of girls in the Science stream

**💡 What I'll use:** Girls in Science = 50% × 720

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Science students = 60% × 1200 = 720

Step 2: Girls in Science = 50% × 720 = 360

**✅ Answer: 360 girls**

---

### Q3

**❓ Question:** How many boys are in Commerce?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Commerce = 300 students; 40% are girls → 60% are boys
- Find: Count of boys in Commerce

**💡 What I'll use:** Boys = (100% − Girls%) × Commerce count

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Commerce students = 25% × 1200 = 300

Step 2: Girls in Commerce = 40% × 300 = 120

Step 3: Boys in Commerce = 300 − 120 = 180

(Or directly: Boys = 60% × 300 = 180)

**✅ Answer: 180 boys**

---

### Q4

**❓ Question:** What % of total students are girls?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Girls in each stream as calculated above
- Find: Total girls as a percentage of 1200

**💡 What I'll use:** Sum up all girls, then (Total girls / Total students) × 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Girls in each stream:
- Science: 50% × 720 = 360
- Commerce: 40% × 300 = 120
- Arts: 30% × 180 = 54

Step 2: Total girls = 360 + 120 + 54 = 534

Step 3: Percentage = (534 / 1200) × 100 = 44.5%

**✅ Answer: 44.5%**

## 📋 Caselet 2: City Population

**Given:** A city has a population of 5 lakhs. 40% are below 30 years, 35% are 30–60 years, and the rest are above 60 years. Literacy rate: 80% for below 30, 75% for 30–60, 50% for above 60.

**Extracted Data:**
| Age Group | % Share | Population  | Literacy % | Literate    |
|-----------|---------|-------------|------------|-------------|
| Below 30  | 40%     | 2,00,000    | 80%        | 1,60,000    |
| 30–60     | 35%     | 1,75,000    | 75%        | 1,31,250    |
| Above 60  | 25%     | 1,25,000    | 50%        | 62,500      |
| **Total** |         | **5,00,000**|            | **3,53,750**|

---

### Q5

**❓ Question:** How many people are above 60 years?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Total = 5 lakhs; Below 30 = 40%, 30–60 = 35%, Above 60 = rest
- Find: Count of people above 60

**💡 What I'll use:** Above 60 % = 100 − 40 − 35 = 25%; then Amount = 25% × 5,00,000

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Above 60 % = 100 − 40 − 35 = 25%

Step 2: Above 60 population = (25 / 100) × 5,00,000 = 1,25,000

**✅ Answer: 1,25,000 people**

---

### Q6

**❓ Question:** How many people below 30 are literate?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Below 30 population = 2,00,000; literacy rate = 80%
- Find: Number of literate people in the below-30 age group

**💡 What I'll use:** Literate count = Literacy% × Population of that group

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Below 30 population = 40% × 5,00,000 = 2,00,000

Step 2: Literate below 30 = 80% × 2,00,000 = 1,60,000

**✅ Answer: 1,60,000 people**

---

### Q7

**❓ Question:** What is the total literate population?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Literacy rates and populations for each age group
- Find: Sum of all literate people across all three groups

**💡 What I'll use:** Compute literate count for each group, then add

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Literate below 30 = 80% × 2,00,000 = 1,60,000

Step 2: Literate 30–60 = 75% × 1,75,000 = 1,31,250

Step 3: Literate above 60 = 50% × 1,25,000 = 62,500

Step 4: Total literate = 1,60,000 + 1,31,250 + 62,500 = 3,53,750

**✅ Answer: 3,53,750**
