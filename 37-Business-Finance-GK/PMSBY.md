# PMSBY

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) is an accident insurance scheme. Coverage is **₹2 lakh** for accidental death/full disability.

1. PMSBY primarily provides:  
   A) Pension B) Accident insurance C) Crop loan D) Education loan  
   **Answer: B**
2. Maximum coverage under PMSBY is:  
   A) ₹50,000 B) ₹1 lakh C) ₹2 lakh D) ₹5 lakh  
   **Answer: C**
3. PMSBY is generally renewed on:  
   A) Monthly basis B) Half-yearly C) Yearly basis D) Once in lifetime  
   **Answer: C**
