# Bond Valuation

Bond value is the present value of coupon payments plus present value of face value.

1. If market interest rate rises, bond price generally:  
   A) Rises B) Falls C) Stays fixed always D) Doubles  
   **Answer: B**
2. Bond valuation is based on:  
   A) Historical cost only B) Present value of future cash flows C) Random walk D) Inventory turnover  
   **Answer: B**
