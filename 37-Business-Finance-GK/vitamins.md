# Vitamins

Vitamins are essential micronutrients; deficiency questions are common in GK.

1. Deficiency of Vitamin C causes:  
   A) Rickets B) Scurvy C) Night blindness D) Goiter  
   **Answer: B**
2. Vitamin D deficiency is associated with:  
   A) Rickets B) Scurvy C) Beriberi D) Pellagra  
   **Answer: A**
3. Night blindness is linked to deficiency of:  
   A) Vitamin A B) Vitamin K C) Vitamin B12 D) Vitamin E  
   **Answer: A**
