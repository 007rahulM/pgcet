# Health Belief Model (HBM)

HBM explains health behavior using perceived susceptibility, severity, benefits, barriers, cues to action, and self-efficacy.

1. Which is a core HBM component?  
   A) GDP growth B) Perceived severity C) Repo rate D) Balance sheet  
   **Answer: B**
2. A reminder SMS for vaccination is an example of:  
   A) Barrier B) Cue to action C) Liability D) Moral hazard  
   **Answer: B**
