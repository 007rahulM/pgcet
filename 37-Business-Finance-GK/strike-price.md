# Strike Price

Strike price is the pre-agreed price at which an option buyer can buy/sell the underlying asset.

1. In a call option, strike price is the price at which buyer can:  
   A) Sell stock B) Buy stock C) Lend money D) Close account  
   **Answer: B**
2. If market price > strike price for a call, option is generally:  
   A) Out of money B) At par C) In the money D) Illegal  
   **Answer: C**
