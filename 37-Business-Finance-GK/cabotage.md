# Cabotage

Cabotage refers to transport of goods/passengers between two points within one country, often restricted to domestic carriers.

1. Cabotage in shipping/aviation mainly concerns:  
   A) International treaty tax B) Domestic point-to-point transport rights C) Online payments D) Railway fares  
   **Answer: B**
2. Cabotage relaxation usually aims to improve:  
   A) Capacity and connectivity B) Pollution C) Import duty D) Census process  
   **Answer: A**
