# Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship is creating and managing a venture by taking risk and innovating to create value.

1. Entrepreneur is mainly a:  
   A) Passive investor B) Risk-taking venture creator C) Government auditor D) Tax collector  
   **Answer: B**
2. Startup ecosystem supports entrepreneurs through:  
   A) Incubation and mentorship B) Food subsidy only C) Weather alerts D) Passport services  
   **Answer: A**
