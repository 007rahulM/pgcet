# Competitive Advantage

Competitive advantage means a firm’s ability to perform better than rivals through cost, quality, brand, tech, or distribution.

1. Which is a source of competitive advantage?  
   A) Higher wastage B) Better supply chain C) Delayed service D) Random pricing  
   **Answer: B**
2. Sustainable competitive advantage is one that is:  
   A) Easy to copy B) Temporary C) Difficult to imitate D) Not profitable  
   **Answer: C**
