# Inclusive Workplace

An inclusive workplace ensures equal respect, opportunity, and participation across gender, disability, culture, and background.

1. Inclusion policy mainly promotes:  
   A) Exclusion B) Equal participation C) Monopoly D) Price control  
   **Answer: B**
2. Accessibility features at office are linked to:  
   A) Inclusion B) Inflation C) Cabotage D) Arbitrage  
   **Answer: A**
