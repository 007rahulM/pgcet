# Xiaomi and Foxconn

Foxconn is a major electronics contract manufacturer; companies like Xiaomi use such partners for large-scale manufacturing/assembly.

1. Foxconn is best known as a:  
   A) Social media firm B) Contract electronics manufacturer C) Mutual fund D) Insurer  
   **Answer: B**
2. Xiaomi–Foxconn relationship is mainly:  
   A) Banking partnership B) Manufacturing/assembly linkage C) Airline alliance D) Pharma licensing  
   **Answer: B**
