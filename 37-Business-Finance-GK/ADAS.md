# ADAS

ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) includes safety technologies like lane assist, adaptive cruise control, and automatic emergency braking.

1. ADAS stands for:  
   A) Automated Data Analytics Suite B) Advanced Driver Assistance Systems C) Advanced Digital Asset Security D) Automatic Driving Audit Standard  
   **Answer: B**
2. Which is an ADAS feature?  
   A) Lane departure warning B) Tax deduction C) Crop insurance D) Bond coupon reset  
   **Answer: A**
