# NCTE Functions

NCTE (National Council for Teacher Education) regulates standards and recognition for teacher education institutions in India.

1. NCTE is mainly related to:  
   A) Medical licensing B) Teacher education standards C) Tax appeals D) Airport regulation  
   **Answer: B**
2. One major role of NCTE is:  
   A) Approving stock IPOs B) Recognizing teacher training institutions C) Running elections D) Setting repo rate  
   **Answer: B**
