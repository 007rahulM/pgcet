# Problems on Ages — Solutions

1. Let ages \(4x,5x\).  
   \(\frac{4x+6}{5x+6}=\frac{5}{6}\Rightarrow 24x+36=25x+30\Rightarrow x=6\).  
   **Answer: 24 years and 30 years**

2. Let son \(=x\), father \(=4x\).  
   \(4x+8=3(x+8)\Rightarrow x=16\).  
   **Answer: Son 16, Father 64**

3. Sum increases by 8 in 4 years for two persons.  
   **Answer: 52**

4. Let child \(=x\), mother \(=x+26\).  
   \(x+30=3(x+4)\Rightarrow x=9\).  
   **Answer: Child 9, Mother 35**

5. Let B \(=x\), A \(=x+6\).  
   5 years ago: \(x+1=2(x-5)\Rightarrow x=11\).  
   **Answer: B 11, A 17**
