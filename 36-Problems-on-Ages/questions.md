# Problems on Ages — Practice Questions

1. Present ages of A and B are in ratio 4:5. After 6 years, ratio becomes 5:6. Find present ages.
2. Father is 4 times son. After 8 years, father is 3 times son. Find present ages.
3. Sum of ages of two persons is 44. After 4 years, sum will be?
4. Mother is 26 years older than child. After 4 years, mother will be 3 times child. Find ages now.
5. A is 6 years older than B. 5 years ago, A was twice B. Find present ages.
