## 📐 TOPIC 1: Time & Work (PYQs)

**2023-Q1:** A can do a work in 12 days, B in 18 days. Together?
**Answer:** 7.2 days → LCM=36, A=3, B=2, Together=5, Time=36/5

**2021-Q (T&W):** Pipes A and B fill tank in 20 and 30 min. Together?
**Answer:** 12 min → LCM=60, A=3, B=2, Together=5, Time=60/5

**2020-Q (Pattern):** A alone 15 days, B alone 20 days. A works 5 days alone, then both finish. Total time?
**Solution:**
- LCM=60, A=4, B=3
- Work by A in 5 days = 20 units
- Remaining = 40 units
- Together rate = 7/day
- Time = 40/7 days
- Total = 5 + 40/7 = 75/7 days ≈ 10.7 days

## 📐 TOPIC 9: Mathematical Logic (PYQs)

**2021-Q10:** Logical equivalent of p → q?
**Answer:** ¬q → ¬p (contrapositive)

**2022-Q7:** p=T, q=F. p→q = ?
**Answer:** False

**2020 (Pattern):** De Morgan: ¬(A∧B) = ?
**Answer:** ¬A ∨ ¬B

**Answer:** Yes — always true
