# Ranking and Position-Based Reasoning — Practice Problem Solutions

### Problem 1

**❓ Question:** In a class of 40, Riya is 9th from top. What is her rank from bottom?

**✏️ Solution:**  
Bottom rank \(= 40 - 9 + 1 = 32\)

**✅ Answer:** 32nd from bottom

---

### Problem 2

**❓ Question:** In a class of 52, Anil is 17th from bottom. What is his rank from top?

**✏️ Solution:**  
Top rank \(= 52 - 17 + 1 = 36\)

**✅ Answer:** 36th from top

---

### Problem 3

**❓ Question:** P is 12th from top and Q is 19th from bottom in a class of 45. How many students are between them?

**✏️ Solution:**  
Q from top \(= 45 - 19 + 1 = 27\)  
Students between \(= 27 - 12 - 1 = 14\)

**✅ Answer:** 14 students
