# Change in Compounding Period — Practice Problem Solutions

## 1) ₹100000 at 8% p.a. for 2 years — compare annual and half-yearly amounts.
- Annual = 100000 × 1.08² = ₹116640
- Half-yearly = 100000 × 1.04⁴ = ₹116985.86
- Difference = **₹345.86**

## 2) ₹40000 at 12% p.a. for 1 year — compare annual and quarterly amounts.
- Annual = 40000 × 1.12 = ₹44800
- Quarterly = 40000 × 1.03⁴ = ₹45020.35
- Difference = **₹220.35**

## 3) ₹60000 at 10% p.a. for 2 years — how much is saved if annual compounding is used instead of half-yearly?
- Annual = 60000 × 1.10² = ₹72600
- Half-yearly = 60000 × 1.05⁴ = ₹72930.38
- Saved = **₹330.38**

## 4) ₹50000 at 6% p.a. for 2 years — compare SI and annual CI.
- SI = 50000 × 6 × 2 / 100 = ₹6000
- CI = 50000 × 1.06² − 50000 = ₹6180
- Difference = **₹180**

## 5) ₹200000 at 10% p.a. for 1 year — how much more does half-yearly compounding give than annual?
- Annual amount = ₹220000
- Half-yearly amount = 200000 × 1.05² = ₹220500
- Difference = **₹500**
