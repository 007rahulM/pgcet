# Simple Interest — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** P = ₹2000, R = 5%, T = 4 years. Find SI.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Principal = ₹2000, Rate = 5% per year, Time = 4 years
- Find: Simple Interest

**💡 What I'll use:** SI = (P × R × T) / 100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Substitute all values
SI = (2000 × 5 × 4) / 100 = 40000 / 100 = **₹400**

**✅ Answer: SI = ₹400**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** SI = ₹360, P = ₹1200, T = 3 years. Find R.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: SI = ₹360, Principal = ₹1200, Time = 3 years
- Find: Rate of interest

**💡 What I'll use:** R = (SI × 100) / (P × T)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Substitute values
R = (360 × 100) / (1200 × 3) = 36000 / 3600 = **10%**

**✅ Answer: Rate = 10%**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** At what rate will ₹1500 give SI = ₹225 in 3 years?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: P = ₹1500, SI = ₹225, T = 3 years
- Find: Rate of interest

**💡 What I'll use:** R = (SI × 100) / (P × T)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply the formula
R = (225 × 100) / (1500 × 3) = 22500 / 4500 = **5%**

**✅ Answer: Rate = 5%**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** Find the time for ₹800 to grow to ₹1200 at 10% SI.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: P = ₹800, final amount = ₹1200, R = 10%
- Find: Time in years

**💡 What I'll use:** First find SI from the amount, then use T = (SI × 100) / (P × R)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Find SI
SI = Amount − P = 1200 − 800 = ₹400

Step 2: Find Time
T = (400 × 100) / (800 × 10) = 40000 / 8000 = **5 years**

**✅ Answer: 5 years**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** What sum at 8% per year for 5 years gives SI of ₹600?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: SI = ₹600, R = 8%, T = 5 years
- Find: Principal

**💡 What I'll use:** P = (SI × 100) / (R × T)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Substitute values
P = (600 × 100) / (8 × 5) = 60000 / 40 = **₹1500**

**✅ Answer: Principal = ₹1500**
