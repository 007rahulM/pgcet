# Simple Interest — Complete Guide

## 🔍 When This Comes Up

- "Find Simple Interest on ₹X at Y% for Z years"
- "At what rate/time will amount reach ₹X?"
- "Find principal if SI and rate/time are given"

---

## 📐 Core Formula

> **SI = (P × R × T) / 100**
> **Amount = P + SI**

Where:
- P = Principal (initial amount)
- R = Rate of interest (per year %)
- T = Time (in years)

---

## 🔄 Derived Formulas

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| SI | (P × R × T) / 100 |
| Principal (P) | (SI × 100) / (R × T) |
| Rate (R) | (SI × 100) / (P × T) |
| Time (T) | (SI × 100) / (P × R) |

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step Examples

### Example 1 (Find SI and Amount)

**Problem:** ₹5000 invested at 8% per year for 3 years. Find SI and Amount.

- SI = 5000 × 8 × 3 / 100 = **₹1200**
- Amount = 5000 + 1200 = **₹6200** ✅

---

### Example 2 (Find Principal)

**Problem:** SI = ₹900, Rate = 6%, Time = 5 years. Find Principal.

- P = (SI × 100) / (R × T) = (900 × 100) / (6 × 5) = **₹3000** ✅

---

### Example 3 (Find Rate)

**Problem:** P = ₹1500, SI = ₹225, T = 3 years. Find rate.

- R = (225 × 100) / (1500 × 3) = **5%** ✅

---

### Example 4 (Find Time)

**Problem:** At what time will ₹3000 at 5% SI become ₹3750?

- SI = 3750 − 3000 = 750
- T = (SI × 100)/(P × R) = (750 × 100)/(3000 × 5) = **5 years** ✅

---

### Example 5 (Sum triples)

**Problem:** A sum triples at SI in 20 years. Find rate.

- If sum triples, SI = 2P (amount = 3P, so SI = amount − P = 2P)
- R = (2P × 100) / (P × 20) = **10%** ✅

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Shortcuts

| Need | Shortcut |
|------|---------|
| SI for 2 years | 2PR/100 |
| Years to double at SI | 100/R |
| Rate if amount = nP in T years | (n−1) × 100 / T |

---

## 📝 Practice Problems

1. P = ₹2000, R = 5%, T = 4 years. Find SI.

2. SI = ₹360, P = ₹1200, T = 3 years. Find R.

3. At what rate will ₹1500 give SI = ₹225 in 3 years?

4. Find the time for ₹800 to grow to ₹1200 at 10% SI.

5. What sum at 8% per year for 5 years gives SI of ₹600?

---

## ✔️ Answers

1. 2000 × 5 × 4 / 100 = **₹400**
2. R = (360 × 100)/(1200 × 3) = **10%**
3. R = (225 × 100)/(1500 × 3) = **5%**
4. SI = 400. T = (400 × 100)/(800 × 10) = **5 years**
5. P = (600 × 100)/(8 × 5) = **₹1500**
