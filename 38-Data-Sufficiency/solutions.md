# Data Sufficiency — Solutions

1. A gives only R year; B gives S year; together compare 2001 vs 2003.  
   **Answer: 3 (Both together sufficient)**

2. A alone gives siblings but not gender; B alone gives gender but no relation; together enough.  
   **Answer: 3 (Both together sufficient)**

3. A alone already gives mapping for C, A, T.  
   **Answer: 1 (A alone sufficient)**

4. A alone directly gives x>y.  
   **Answer: 1 (A alone sufficient)**

5. A gives relation, B gives son age; together father age = 36.  
   **Answer: 3 (Both together sufficient)**
