# Pattern 2: Number Analogy

## 🔍 How to Recognize This Pattern

- "4 : 16 :: 5 : ?"
- "3 : 27 :: 4 : ?"
- "36 : 6 :: 49 : ?"
- Number pairs with a mathematical pattern

---

## 🧠 The Golden Rule

> **Find the mathematical operation linking the first pair. Apply it to the second pair.**

---

## 📐 Common Number Relationship Types

| Type | First Pair | Pattern | Second Pair (Answer) |
|------|-----------|---------|---------------------|
| **Square** | 4 : 16 | 4² = 16 | 5 : 25 |
| **Cube** | 2 : 8 | 2³ = 8 | 3 : 27 |
| **Square Root** | 36 : 6 | √36 = 6 | 49 : 7 |
| **Double** | 5 : 10 | 5 × 2 = 10 | 8 : 16 |
| **Triple** | 3 : 9 | 3 × 3 = 9 | 4 : 12 |
| **Add constant** | 7 : 11 | 7 + 4 = 11 | 9 : 13 |
| **Subtract** | 15 : 8 | 15 − 7 = 8 | 20 : 13 |
| **Product** | 4 : 20 | 4 × 5 = 20 | 6 : 30 |
| **Reverse digits** | 23 : 32 | reverse | 45 : 54 |
| **Sum of digits** | 14 : 5 | 1+4 = 5 | 23 : 5 |
| **n²+1** | 3 : 10 | 3²+1 = 10 | 4 : 17 |
| **n²−1** | 4 : 15 | 4²−1 = 15 | 5 : 24 |

## ✅ Step-by-Step Examples

### Example 1

**❓ Question:** 4 : 16 :: 7 : ?
- (A) 21  (B) 28  (C) 49  (D) 14

**My Solution:**
- 4 : 16 → 4² = 16 (square)
- 7 : ? → 7² = 49

**✅ Answer: (C) 49**

---

### Example 2

**❓ Question:** 3 : 27 :: 4 : ?
- (A) 16  (B) 64  (C) 48  (D) 32

**My Solution:**
- 3 : 27 → 3³ = 27 (cube)
- 4 : ? → 4³ = 64

**✅ Answer: (B) 64**

---

### Example 3

**❓ Question:** 5 : 30 :: 7 : ?
- (A) 42  (B) 49  (C) 35  (D) 56

**My Solution:**
- 5 : 30 → 5 × 6 = 30
- Try: 5 × (5+1) = 30 → n × (n+1)
- 7 : ? → 7 × (7+1) = 7 × 8 = 56

**✅ Answer: (D) 56**

---

### Example 4

**❓ Question:** 121 : 11 :: 169 : ?
- (A) 12  (B) 13  (C) 14  (D) 15

**My Solution:**
- 121 : 11 → √121 = 11 (square root)
- 169 : ? → √169 = 13

**✅ Answer: (B) 13**

---

### Example 5

**❓ Question:** 2 : 10 :: 3 : 29 (what's the pattern?)
OR simpler: 3 : 10 :: 5 : ?
- (A) 26  (B) 28  (C) 24  (D) 30

**My Solution:**
- 3 : 10 → 3² + 1 = 10 (square + 1)
- 5 : ? → 5² + 1 = 26

**✅ Answer: (A) 26**

---

### Example 6

**❓ Question:** 14 : 5 :: 23 : ?
- (A) 4  (B) 5  (C) 6  (D) 7

**My Solution:**
- 14 : 5 → 1 + 4 = 5 (sum of digits)
- 23 : ? → 2 + 3 = 5

**✅ Answer: (B) 5**

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Method

1. Try **square** first (most common): Is second = first²?
2. Try **cube**: Is second = first³?
3. Try **multiply by constant**: second ÷ first = constant?
4. Try **add/subtract constant**: second − first = constant?
5. Try **square ± constant**: Is second = first² ± 1, ± 2?
6. Try **sum of digits** or **reverse**

## 📝 Practice Problems

**Q1.** 6 : 36 :: 9 : ?
- (A) 81  (B) 18  (C) 45  (D) 54

**Q2.** 5 : 125 :: 4 : ?
- (A) 16  (B) 64  (C) 48  (D) 32

**Q3.** 7 : 50 :: 6 : ?
- (A) 36  (B) 37  (C) 42  (D) 35

**Q4.** 25 : 5 :: 81 : ?
- (A) 8  (B) 9  (C) 18  (D) 27

**Q5.** 3 : 12 :: 5 : ?
- (A) 20  (B) 25  (C) 30  (D) 35

---

## ✔️ Answers

| Q | Answer | Reason |
|---|--------|--------|
| Q1 | (A) 81 | 6² = 36 → 9² = 81 |
| Q2 | (B) 64 | 5³ = 125 → 4³ = 64 |
| Q3 | (B) 37 | 7²+1 = 50 → 6²+1 = 37 |
| Q4 | (B) 9 | √25 = 5 → √81 = 9 |
| Q5 | (C) 30 | 3 × 4 = 12 → 5 × 6 = 30 (n × (n+1)) |
