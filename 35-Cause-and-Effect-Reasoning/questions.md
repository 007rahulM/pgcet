# Cause and Effect — Practice Questions

For each pair, choose:
- A: I is cause, II is effect
- B: II is cause, I is effect
- C: Both are independent causes
- D: Both are effects of a common cause

1. I. A truck overturned on a highway. II. Police cordoned off the area.
2. I. Heavy rain occurred overnight. II. Several low-lying roads were flooded.
3. I. The company increased product price. II. Sales volume dropped next month.
4. I. Students performed poorly in exam. II. Many classes were cancelled before exam.
5. I. Electricity bill rose sharply. II. Summer temperature increased unusually.
6. I. Government announced tax rebate for EVs. II. EV purchases increased.
7. I. A bank server outage happened. II. Online transactions failed for two hours.
8. I. The city reported dengue cases rise. II. Stagnant water was seen in many areas.
9. I. Interest rates were reduced. II. Home loan applications increased.
10. I. Traffic congestion increased near market. II. Festival season shopping started.
11. I. A factory released untreated waste. II. Fish deaths were reported in river.
12. I. A new competitor entered market. II. Existing firm increased advertisement spend.
