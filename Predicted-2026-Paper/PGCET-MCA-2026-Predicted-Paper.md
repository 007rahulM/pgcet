## 📝 DETAILED SOLUTIONS (Key Questions)

### Q5. Salary increase by 20% then decrease by 20%
- Start: 100
- After 20% increase: 100 × 1.2 = 120
- After 20% decrease: 120 × 0.8 = 96
- Net: **4% decrease** ✅ (Answer: B)

### Q9. Mark up 40%, discount 20%
- Assume CP = 100
- Marked Price = 100 × 1.4 = 140
- SP after 20% discount = 140 × 0.8 = 112
- Profit% = (112−100)/100 × 100 = **12%** ✅ (Answer: C)

### Q23. Coefficients of three consecutive terms in (1 + x)ⁿ are in AP
- Condition: 2·C(n, r) = C(n, r−1) + C(n, r+1)
- Solving gives **n = 55** ✅ (Answer: C)

### Q24. Sum = 8 when two dice rolled
- Favorable: (2,6),(3,5),(4,4),(5,3),(6,2) = **5 outcomes**
- P = 5/36 ✅ (Answer: B)

### Q33. De Morgan: ¬(p ∧ q) = ¬p ∨ ¬q ✅ (Answer: B)

### Q36. 10111₂ = 16+4+2+1 = **23** ✅ (Answer: B)

### Q44. Stack — insertion and deletion only at one end (top) ✅ (Answer: B)

### Q45. Stack operations:
- push(A): [A]
- push(B): [A, B]
- push(C): [A, B, C]
- pop(): removes C → [A, B]
- push(D): [A, B, D]
- pop(): removes D → [A, B]
- Top = **B** ✅ (Answer: B)

### Q46. Complete binary tree with 15 nodes:
- Levels (1-indexed): 1, 2, 4, 8 → **4 levels** → height = **4** ✅ (Answer: B)

### Q58. `x++` is post-increment — prints value BEFORE increment
- Prints **10**, then x becomes 11 ✅ (Answer: A)

### Q68. 5, 11, 23, 47, ?
- 5×2+1=11, 11×2+1=23, 23×2+1=47, **47×2+1=95** ✅ (Answer: B)

### Q71–Q73. Seating arrangement (6 people)
- Clockwise arrangement: **A → B → C → D → F → E**
  - A opposite D
  - B right of A
  - C between B and D
  - E left of A
  - F opposite B
- Q71: Opposite A = **D** ✅ (A)
- Q72: Immediate left of D = **C** ✅ (B)
- Q73: Between E and B = **A** ✅ (C)
