## Why It Appears Every Year

Looking at real PGCET papers:
- **2023:** Ellipse foci, Circle center/radius, Parabola equation, Point dividing line segment
- **2024:** Ellipse eccentricity, tangent to curves, angle between curves
- **2025:** Circle through 2 points, Ellipse equation, Hyperbola foci, midpoint in 3D, angle between 3D lines

## Official Syllabus Coverage

> Rectangular Cartesian co-ordinates, Equations of Line, Midpoint, Intersections, Equations of a Circle, Distance formulae, Pair of straight lines, **Parabola, Ellipse and Hyperbola**, Simple Geometric transformations such as Translation, Rotation, Scaling.
