# Ratio & Proportion: Proportion Concepts — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** Find x: 4:x = 8:14

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A proportion 4:x = 8:14
- Find: The unknown value x

**💡 What I'll use:** Cross-multiplication property: a × d = b × c

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Cross-multiply
4 × 14 = 8 × x
56 = 8x

Step 2: Solve for x
x = 56 / 8 = **7**

**✅ Answer: x = 7**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** Find mean proportional between 9 and 16.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Two numbers 9 and 16
- Find: A number m such that 9:m = m:16

**💡 What I'll use:** Mean proportional = √(product of extremes) = √(a × b)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply the formula
m = √(9 × 16) = √144 = **12**

Step 2: Verify: 9:12 = 12:16 → 3:4 = 3:4 ✓

**✅ Answer: 12**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** Find the fourth proportional to 2, 5, 6.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Three numbers 2, 5, 6; find x such that 2:5 = 6:x
- Find: The fourth proportional x

**💡 What I'll use:** a:b = c:d → a × d = b × c

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Set up the proportion
2 : 5 = 6 : x

Step 2: Cross-multiply
2 × x = 5 × 6
2x = 30
x = **15**

**✅ Answer: 15**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** If 8 pens cost ₹120, what do 15 pens cost?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 8 pens = ₹120; direct proportion (more pens → more cost)
- Find: Cost of 15 pens

**💡 What I'll use:** Direct proportion: cost per pen × number of pens

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Find cost per pen
Cost per pen = 120 / 8 = ₹15

Step 2: Find cost of 15 pens
Cost = 15 × 15 = **₹225**

**✅ Answer: ₹225**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** A car travels 180 km in 3 hours. How long to travel 300 km at same speed?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 180 km takes 3 hours; speed is constant (direct proportion: more distance → more time)
- Find: Time for 300 km

**💡 What I'll use:** Direct proportion: time = (3 / 180) × 300

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Find time per km
Time per km = 3 / 180 hours

Step 2: Time for 300 km
Time = (3 / 180) × 300 = 900 / 180 = **5 hours**

**✅ Answer: 5 hours**
