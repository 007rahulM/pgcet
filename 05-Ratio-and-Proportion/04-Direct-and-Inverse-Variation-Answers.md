# Direct and Inverse Variation — Practice Problem Solutions

## 1) If 18 kg of rice fills 3 bags equally, how much rice is needed for 5 bags?
- Per bag = 18/3 = 6 kg
- For 5 bags = **30 kg**

## 2) 6 workers take 14 days. How many days will 7 workers take?
- 6 × 14 = 7 × x
- x = 84/7 = **12 days**

## 3) 9 books cost ₹315. What do 4 books cost?
- Per book = 315/9 = 35
- Cost of 4 books = **₹140**

## 4) If 15 pumps empty a tank in 8 hours, how long will 10 pumps take?
- 15 × 8 = 10 × x
- x = 120/10 = **12 hours**

## 5) If 7 litres of paint cover 56 m², how much area will 9 litres cover?
- Per litre = 56/7 = 8
- Area with 9 litres = **72 m²**
