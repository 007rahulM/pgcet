# Pattern 1: Grammar — Tenses, Articles, Prepositions, Subject-Verb Agreement

## 🔍 When This Comes Up

- "Choose the correct preposition..."

---

## 📐 Part 1: Tenses Quick Guide

| Tense | When | Signal Words | Example |
|-------|------|-------------|---------|
| Simple Present | Habits, facts | always, usually, every day | I eat rice. |
| Present Continuous | Happening now | now, at present, currently | I am eating. |
| Present Perfect | Completed, has effect now | just, already, yet, since, for | I have eaten. |
| Simple Past | Completed action | yesterday, ago, last | I ate rice. |
| Past Continuous | Was happening at a time | while, when | I was eating. |
| Simple Future | Will happen | tomorrow, next | I will eat. |
| Future Perfect | Will be completed before another future event | by the time | I will have finished. |

---

## 📐 Part 2: Articles (a, an, the)

- **a** — before **consonant sounds**: a book, a university ('yoo' sound = consonant)
- **an** — before **vowel sounds**: an apple, an hour (h is silent), an MBA
- **the** — specific/known thing: the sun, the first boy, the United States
- **No article** — general/abstract: Love is blind. Water is essential.

**Tricky cases:**
- a university ✅ (sounds like 'yoo-niversity' = consonant sound)
- an hour ✅ (h is silent, starts with vowel sound 'ow')
- an MBA ✅ (M sounds like 'em' = vowel sound)
- the United States ✅ (unique country with states)

---

## 📐 Part 3: Prepositions

| Preposition | Use | Example |
|-------------|-----|---------|
| at | specific time/place | at 5 PM, at school |
| in | enclosed space/periods | in the box, in July, in 2020 |
| on | surface/day | on the table, on Monday |
| since | from a specific point in time | since 2020, since Monday |
| for | duration | for 3 hours, for two years |
| by | deadline/agent | by Friday, by train |
| with | accompaniment/instrument | with a pen, with friends |
| to | direction/recipient | go to school, give to her |
| about | topic | talk about, book about |
| of | belonging/quantity | part of, cup of tea |

---

## 📐 Part 4: Subject-Verb Agreement

| Subject | Verb |
|---------|------|
| I / You / We / They | plural verb: **run, are** |
| He / She / It | singular verb: **runs, is** |
| Uncountable noun (water, music) | singular verb: **is, has** |
| Collective noun (team, family) | singular verb: **is playing** |
| Either/Neither + singular | singular verb |
| "None of" | usually singular |

## ✅ Step-by-Step Examples

### Example 1 (Article)
**Q:** "I want to be ___ engineer."
- "engineer" starts with vowel sound 'e' → use **an**
- **Answer: an** ✅

### Example 2 (Preposition)
**Q:** "I have been waiting ___ 3 hours."
- 3 hours = duration → use **for**
- **Answer: for** ✅

### Example 3 (Tense)
**Q:** "By the time you arrive, I _____ the work."
- "By the time" + future → **Future Perfect**: will + have + past participle
- **Answer: will have finished** ✅

### Example 4 (Subject-Verb Agreement)
**Q:** "Neither of the boys _____ present."
- Neither = singular → singular verb
- **Answer: was** (not "were") ✅

### Example 5 (Tricky Article)
**Q:** "He is ___ MBA graduate."
- MBA starts with 'em' sound (vowel sound) → **an**
- **Answer: an MBA graduate** ✅

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Tips

**Article rule:** Listen to the SOUND, not the spelling.
- "a" before consonant SOUNDS (even if vowel letter): a university, a one-time offer
- "an" before vowel SOUNDS (even if consonant letter): an hour, an MBA

**Preposition rule:**
- Since + specific POINT in time (since 2020)
- For + DURATION (for 3 years)

**Tense signal words:**
- "already, just, yet, since, for" → Present Perfect
- "yesterday, ago, last" → Simple Past
- "by the time, before" + future → Future Perfect

## 📝 Practice Problems

1. Choose the correct form: "She _____ to school every day." (go/goes/going/gone)

2. Fill in article: "I want to be ___ engineer." (a/an/the/no article)

3. Choose preposition: "I have been here ___ Monday." (since/for/from/during)

4. Fill in article: "I have ___ umbrella." (a/an/the)

5. Choose correct verb: "The team _____ playing well." (is/are)

6. Find the error: "She is angry on him."

7. Fill in article: "He is ___ honest man." (a/an/the)

8. Choose tense: "By tomorrow, I _____ this book." (will read/will have read/read)

9. Find the error: "I don't know nothing about this."

10. Choose preposition: "She has been studying here ___ 3 years." (since/for)

---

## ✔️ Answers

1. **(B) goes** — She is 3rd person singular, simple present → "goes"
2. **(B) an** — "engineer" starts with vowel sound 'e'
3. **(A) since** — Monday = specific point in time
4. **(B) an** — "umbrella" starts with vowel sound 'u'
5. **(A) is** — collective noun "team" takes singular verb
6. Error: "angry **on**" → correct: "angry **with** him"
7. **(B) an** — "honest" starts with silent 'h', vowel sound 'o' → "an honest man"
8. **(B) will have read** — Future Perfect for action completed before another future time
9. Error: double negative ("don't" + "nothing") → "I don't know **anything**" OR "I know **nothing**"
10. **(B) for** — 3 years = duration
