# Vocabulary — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** Synonym of "eloquent": (A) Quiet (B) Articulate (C) Confused (D) Rude

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: The word "eloquent" with 4 options
- Find: Which option means the same as eloquent?

**💡 What I'll use:** Eliminate wrong options — eloquent = fluent, expressive, well-spoken

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Eliminate obviously wrong options:
- (A) Quiet: Opposite! Eloquent people speak well, not quietly. ✗
- (C) Confused: Opposite again — confused people can't speak clearly. ✗
- (D) Rude: Eloquent means polished speech, not rudeness. ✗

Step 2: (B) Articulate = able to express ideas clearly and fluently = matches eloquent. ✓

**✅ Answer: (B) Articulate**

### Q2

- Given: Need the OPPOSITE of "frugal"
- Find: Antonym

**💡 What I'll use:** Frugal = careful with money / not wasteful → antonym = wasteful / spendthrift

**✏️ My Solution:**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** One who walks in sleep: (A) Insomniac (B) Somnambulant (C) Somnambulist (D) Hypnotist

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A definition — "one who walks in sleep"
- Find: The one-word substitute

**💡 What I'll use:** Word roots — "somnus" = sleep (Latin), "ambulare" = to walk

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Break down the options:
- (A) Insomniac: insomnia = inability to sleep (not about walking) ✗
- (D) Hypnotist: puts others in a trance-like state, not a sleepwalker ✗
- (B) Somnambulant: somnambulant is the adjective form ✗
- (C) Somnambulist: somnus (sleep) + ambulare (walk) + ist (person who does) = **person who sleepwalks** ✓

**✅ Answer: (C) Somnambulist**

### Q9

**❓ Question:** "Government by one person with absolute power" is: (A) Democracy (B) Oligarchy (C) Autocracy (D) Monarchy

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Definition — government by one person with absolute power
- Find: Correct political term

**💡 What I'll use:** Word roots of each option

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Break down each option:
- (A) Democracy: demos (people) + cracy (rule) = rule by the people ✗
- (B) Oligarchy: oligos (few) + arche (rule) = rule by a few people ✗
- (D) Monarchy: monos (one) + arche (rule) = rule by one, but usually a king/queen (may be constitutional) ✗
- (C) Autocracy: autos (self) + cracy (rule) = self-rule, absolute power by one person ✓

Step 2: "Absolute power" is the key — that's Autocracy specifically.

**✅ Answer: (C) Autocracy**

### Q10

**❓ Question:** Fear of fire is: (A) Pyrophobia (B) Hydrophobia (C) Acrophobia (D) Pyromania

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: "Fear of fire" — need to find the technical term
- Find: Correct phobia name

**💡 What I'll use:** Word roots — "pyro" = fire, "phobia" = fear

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: We need the word for FEAR of fire:
- (B) Hydrophobia: hydro = water → fear of water ✗
- (C) Acrophobia: acro = heights → fear of heights ✗
- (D) Pyromania: pyro = fire + mania = obsession/urge to set fires (not fear!) ✗

Step 2: (A) Pyrophobia: pyro (fire) + phobia (fear) = fear of fire ✓

**✅ Answer: (A) Pyrophobia**
