# Angle Between Hands — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** Angle between hands at 3:00?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: H = 3, M = 0
- Find: Angle

**💡 What I'll use:** Angle = |30H − 5.5M|

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Angle = |30 × 3 − 5.5 × 0| = |90 − 0| = **90°**

**✅ Answer: 90°**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** Between 7 and 8 o'clock, when do the hands coincide?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Time between 7:00 and 8:00; coincidence means angle = 0°
- Find: Exact minute M

**💡 What I'll use:** |30 × 7 − 5.5M| = 0

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: 30 × 7 − 5.5M = 0
        210 = 5.5M
        M = 210/5.5 = 420/11 = **38 2/11 minutes**

Step 2: Hands coincide at **7:38 2/11** (approximately 7:38).

**✅ Answer: 38 2/11 minutes past 7**

### Q7

**❓ Question:** At 8:00, the angle between the hands is:
(A) 240°  (B) 120°  (C) 60°  (D) 180°

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: H = 8, M = 0
- Find: Angle (smaller of the two)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Angle = |30 × 8 − 5.5 × 0| = |240 − 0| = 240°
Step 2: 240° > 180°, so smaller angle = 360° − 240° = **120°**

**✅ Answer: (B) 120°**
