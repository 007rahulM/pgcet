# 3D Geometry — Solutions

1. \(d=\sqrt{(7-3)^2+(1+2)^2+(5-1)^2}=\sqrt{16+9+16}=\sqrt{41}\).  
   **Answer: \(\sqrt{41}\)**

2. Midpoint \(=\left(\frac{-4+2}{2},\frac{6+0}{2},\frac{2+8}{2}\right)=(-1,3,5)\).  
   **Answer: \((-1,3,5)\)**

3. Magnitude \(=\sqrt{4+1+4}=3\).  
   d.c. \(=\left(\frac{2}{3},-\frac{1}{3},\frac{2}{3}\right)\).  
   **Answer: \(\left(\frac{2}{3},-\frac{1}{3},\frac{2}{3}\right)\)**

4. \(\cos\theta=\frac{1\cdot1+0\cdot1+1\cdot0}{\sqrt2\sqrt2}=\frac{1}{2}\).  
   \(\theta=60^\circ\).  
   **Answer: \(60^\circ\)**

5. \(l^2+m^2+n^2=1\Rightarrow 3l^2=1\Rightarrow l=\frac{1}{\sqrt3}\) (positive).  
   **Answer: \(\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt3},\frac{1}{\sqrt3},\frac{1}{\sqrt3}\right)\)**
