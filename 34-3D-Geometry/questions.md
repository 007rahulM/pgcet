# 3D Geometry — Practice Questions

1. Find distance between points \((3,-2,1)\) and \((7,1,5)\).
2. Find midpoint of \((-4,6,2)\) and \((2,0,8)\).
3. For direction ratios \((2,-1,2)\), find direction cosines.
4. Find angle between lines whose direction ratios are \((1,0,1)\) and \((1,1,0)\).
5. If direction cosines are \(l=m=n\) and all positive, find \((l,m,n)\).
