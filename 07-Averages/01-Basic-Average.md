## ЁЯУР Core Formulas

> **Sum = Average ├Ч Number of values**

### Example 1

**тЭУ Question:** Marks of 5 students: 70, 80, 65, 90, 75. Find their average.

**ЁЯдФ What I understood:**
- Given: 5 marks тАФ 70, 80, 65, 90, 75
- Find: The average mark

**ЁЯТб What I'll use:** Average = Sum of all values / Number of values

**тЬПя╕П My Solution:**

**ЁЯТб What I'll use:** Sum = Average ├Ч Count, then subtract the known sum

**тЬПя╕П My Solution:**

**тЬЕ Answer: 6th number = 30**

### Example 3

**тЭУ Question:** Find the average of numbers from 1 to 20.

**ЁЯдФ What I understood:**
- Given: Consecutive integers from 1 to 20
- Find: Their average

**ЁЯТб What I'll use:** Sum of first n naturals = n(n+1)/2, then divide by n; or shortcut: Average = (n+1)/2

**тЬПя╕П My Solution:**

Step 1: Use the sum formula

Step 2: Divide by count

**тЬЕ Answer: Average = 10.5**

### Example 5

**тЭУ Question:** A batsman's average in 10 innings = 40. In the 11th innings he scores 90. What is his new average?

**ЁЯдФ What I understood:**
- Given: Average over 10 innings = 40; 11th innings score = 90
- Find: New average after 11 innings

**ЁЯТб What I'll use:** New Sum = Old Sum + New Score, then divide by new count

**тЬПя╕П My Solution:**

Step 1: Find old total
Old sum = 40 ├Ч 10 = 400

Step 2: Add new score
New sum = 400 + 90 = 490

Step 3: Divide by new count
New average = 490 / 11 = **44.5**

**тЬЕ Answer: New average = 44.5**

## тЪб 60-Second Shortcuts

- **Average of consecutive numbers:** (first + last) / 2
- **Average of first n naturals:** (n+1)/2
- **Average of first n odd numbers:** n
- **Average of first n even numbers:** n+1
