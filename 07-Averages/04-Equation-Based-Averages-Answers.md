# Equation-Based Averages — Practice Problem Solutions

## 1) Out of 8 numbers, 7 are 12 each. The 8th is 4 more than the average. Find the total.
- Let average = x, total = 8x
- 8x = 84 + (x + 4)
- 7x = 88, x = 88/7
- Total = 8x = **704/7 = 100.57**

## 2) In a group of 6 people, 5 spend ₹20 each. The 6th spends ₹5 less than the average. Find the total spend.
- Let average = x, total = 6x
- 6x = 100 + (x − 5)
- 5x = 95, x = 19
- Total = **₹114**

## 3) Average of 9 values is 30. Eight are equal to 28 each. Find the 9th.
- Total = 9 × 30 = 270
- Sum of 8 values = 224
- 9th = **46**

## 4) Average of 12 players is 25. One player scores 7 more than average. The rest 11 have equal scores. Find each of those 11 scores.
- Total = 300
- One player = 32
- Remaining = 268
- Each of 11 = **24.36**

## 5) Average of 5 numbers is 16. Four are equal, and the fifth is 6 more than the average. Find each equal number.
- Total = 80
- Fifth = 22
- Remaining 4 sum = 58
- Each = **14.5**
