### Q1

**❓ Question:** Average of 5 numbers is 30. One number (50) is removed. What is the new average?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 5 numbers, avg = 30; remove the number 50
- Find: New average of remaining 4 numbers

**💡 What I'll use:** New average = (Old sum − Removed value) ÷ New count

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Old sum = 30 × 5 = 150

Step 2: New sum = 150 − 50 = 100

Step 3: New count = 4

Step 4: New average = 100 ÷ 4 = **25**

**✅ Answer: 25**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** Average age of 8 students is 16. A teacher of age 40 joins the group. What is the new average?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 8 students, avg age = 16; teacher aged 40 joins
- Find: New average age of all 9 people

**💡 What I'll use:** New average = (Old sum + New value) ÷ New count

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Old sum = 16 × 8 = 128

Step 2: New sum = 128 + 40 = 168

Step 3: New count = 9

Step 4: New average = 168 ÷ 9 = **18.67** (≈ 18.67)

**✅ Answer: ≈ 18.67**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** Average weight of 20 persons is 60 kg. A person weighing 80 kg is replaced and the average drops to 59 kg. What is the new person's weight?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 20 persons, avg = 60 kg; 80 kg person is replaced; new avg = 59 kg
- Find: Weight of the replacement person

**💡 What I'll use:** New person's weight = Old person's weight − (Drop in total due to average change)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Old sum = 60 × 20 = 1200

Step 2: New sum = 59 × 20 = 1180

Step 3: Decrease in sum = 1200 − 1180 = 20

Step 4: New person's weight = 80 − 20 = **60 kg**

*(The person removed weighed 80 kg; sum dropped by 20; so replacement weighs 80 − 20 = 60 kg)*

**✅ Answer: 60 kg**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** A cricket team of 11 players has an average score of 40. When the captain's score (80) is excluded, what is the new average?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 11 players, avg = 40; captain scored 80 and is excluded
- Find: New average of the remaining 10 players

**💡 What I'll use:** New average = (Old sum − Excluded value) ÷ New count

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Old sum = 40 × 11 = 440

Step 2: New sum = 440 − 80 = 360

Step 3: New count = 10

Step 4: New average = 360 ÷ 10 = **36**

**✅ Answer: 36**
