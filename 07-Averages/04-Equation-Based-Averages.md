# Equation-Based Averages — Hidden Average Conditions

## 🔍 How to Recognize This Pattern

Look for:
- "9 persons spend the same amount; 10th spends x more than average"
- "one value is more/less than the overall average"
- "average is not directly given, but one member is linked to it"
- questions that turn into a **small equation**

---

## 📐 Core Idea

If average = `A` for `n` items,
> **Total sum = nA**

If one member is described as `A + k` or `A - k`, make an equation using total sum.

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step Examples

### Example 1

**❓ Question:** Out of 10 persons, 9 persons spent ₹15 each on breakfast. The 10th person spent ₹9 more than the average expenditure of all 10 persons. Find the total amount spent.

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Let average expenditure = x
- Then total expenditure = 10x

Step 2: Write total using the persons
- 9 persons spent 15 each → 9 × 15 = 135
- 10th person spent x + 9
- So total = 135 + (x + 9)

Step 3: Form the equation
- 10x = 144 + x
- 9x = 144
- x = 16

Step 4: Total = 10x = **₹160**

**✅ Answer: ₹160**

---

### Example 2

**❓ Question:** Average of 8 numbers is 24. Seven of them are 20 each. Find the 8th number.

**✏️ My Solution:**
- Total = 8 × 24 = 192
- Sum of 7 numbers = 7 × 20 = 140
- 8th number = 192 − 140 = **52**

**✅ Answer: 52**

---

### Example 3

**❓ Question:** The average score of 11 players is 42. One player scored 8 more than the average. The remaining 10 players scored equally. Find each of those 10 scores.

**✏️ My Solution:**
- Let average = 42
- Total = 11 × 42 = 462
- One player's score = 42 + 8 = 50
- Remaining 10 total = 462 − 50 = 412
- Each of the 10 players = 412/10 = **41.2**

**✅ Answer: 41.2**

---

### Example 4

**❓ Question:** Average of 6 numbers is 18. Five numbers are equal and the sixth is 8 more than the average. Find each of the equal numbers.

**✏️ My Solution:**
- Total = 6 × 18 = 108
- Sixth number = 18 + 8 = 26
- Sum of 5 equal numbers = 108 − 26 = 82
- Each = 82/5 = **16.4**

**✅ Answer: 16.4**

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Shortcut

- If the sentence says "more than average" or "less than average", let average = **x**
- Immediately write total as **nx**
- Write the same total from the group description
- Solve the one-line equation

**Exam clue:** when one member is linked to the **average itself**, plain add/subtract methods are not enough — use an equation.

3. Average of 9 values is 30. Eight are equal to 28 each. Find the 9th.
4. Average of 12 players is 25. One player scores 7 more than average. The rest 11 have equal scores. Find each of those 11 scores.
5. Average of 5 numbers is 16. Four are equal, and the fifth is 6 more than the average. Find each equal number.
