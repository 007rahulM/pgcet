### Q1

**❓ Question:** Group X has 40 students with an average of 50. Group Y has 60 students with an average of 70. What is the combined average?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Group X — 40 students, avg = 50; Group Y — 60 students, avg = 70
- Find: Combined average of all 100 students

**💡 What I'll use:** Combined average = (n₁×avg₁ + n₂×avg₂) ÷ (n₁ + n₂)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Sum of Group X = 40 × 50 = 2000

Step 2: Sum of Group Y = 60 × 70 = 4200

Step 3: Total sum = 2000 + 4200 = 6200

Step 4: Total count = 40 + 60 = 100

Step 5: Combined average = 6200 ÷ 100 = **62**

**✅ Answer: 62**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** 5 numbers have an average of 30. Another 10 numbers have an average of 15. Find the combined average.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 5 numbers avg = 30; 10 numbers avg = 15
- Find: Average of all 15 numbers

**💡 What I'll use:** Combined average = (n₁×avg₁ + n₂×avg₂) ÷ (n₁ + n₂)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Sum of first group = 5 × 30 = 150

Step 2: Sum of second group = 10 × 15 = 150

Step 3: Total sum = 150 + 150 = 300

Step 4: Total count = 5 + 10 = 15

Step 5: Combined average = 300 ÷ 15 = **20**

**✅ Answer: 20**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** A student scored 75, 80, 65, 90 in 4 subjects. What must they score in the 5th subject to get an average of 78?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Scores 75, 80, 65, 90 in 4 subjects; desired average over 5 subjects = 78
- Find: Required score in the 5th subject

**💡 What I'll use:** Required score = (Desired avg × Total subjects) − Current sum

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Required total sum = 78 × 5 = 390

Step 2: Current sum = 75 + 80 + 65 + 90 = 310

Step 3: Required 5th score = 390 − 310 = **80**

**✅ Answer: 80**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** Average of A and B is 45. Average of C and D is 55. What is the average of all four?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: avg(A, B) = 45; avg(C, D) = 55
- Find: avg(A, B, C, D)

**💡 What I'll use:** Combined average = Total sum ÷ Total count

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Sum of A + B = 45 × 2 = 90

Step 2: Sum of C + D = 55 × 2 = 110

Step 3: Total sum = 90 + 110 = 200

Step 4: Total count = 4

Step 5: Combined average = 200 ÷ 4 = **50**

**✅ Answer: 50**
