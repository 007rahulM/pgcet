### Q1

**❓ Question:** Find the average of 10, 20, 30, 40, 50.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Five numbers — 10, 20, 30, 40, 50
- Find: Their average

**💡 What I'll use:** Average = Sum of all values ÷ Count

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Add all values → 10 + 20 + 30 + 40 + 50 = 150

Step 2: Divide by count → 150 ÷ 5 = **30**

**✅ Answer: 30**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** Average of 30 results is 25. Average of first 16 results is 23 and last 15 is 27. Find the 16th result.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Total 30 results, overall avg = 25; first 16 avg = 23; last 15 avg = 27
- Find: The 16th result (which is shared by both groups)

**💡 What I'll use:** Sum = Average × Count; 16th result = (Sum of first 16) + (Sum of last 15) − Total sum

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Total sum = 25 × 30 = 750

Step 2: Sum of first 16 = 23 × 16 = 368

Step 3: Sum of last 15 = 27 × 15 = 405

Step 4: The 16th result is counted in both groups, so:
16th result = 368 + 405 − 750 = **23**

**✅ Answer: 23**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** Find the average of the first 50 natural numbers.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Natural numbers 1, 2, 3, …, 50
- Find: Their average

**💡 What I'll use:** Average of first n natural numbers = (n + 1) / 2

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply formula → (50 + 1) / 2 = 51 / 2 = **25.5**

*(Verification: Sum = 50 × 51 / 2 = 1275; Average = 1275 / 50 = 25.5 ✓)*

**✅ Answer: 25.5**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** Average of 5 numbers is 20. One number is removed and the average becomes 18. Find the removed number.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 5 numbers, avg = 20; after removing one, 4 numbers remain with avg = 18
- Find: The number that was removed

**💡 What I'll use:** Removed number = Old sum − New sum

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Old sum = 20 × 5 = 100

Step 2: New sum (4 numbers) = 18 × 4 = 72

Step 3: Removed number = 100 − 72 = **28**

**✅ Answer: 28**

---

### Q5

**❓ Question:** A class of 25 students has an average score of 60. Three absent students with scores 70, 80, 90 are added. What is the new average?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: 25 students, avg = 60; 3 new students with scores 70, 80, 90 join
- Find: New average after all 28 students

**💡 What I'll use:** New average = (Old sum + New sum) ÷ New count

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Old sum = 60 × 25 = 1500

Step 2: Sum of 3 new scores = 70 + 80 + 90 = 240

Step 3: New total sum = 1500 + 240 = 1740

Step 4: New count = 25 + 3 = 28

Step 5: New average = 1740 ÷ 28 = **62.14** (≈ 62.14)

**✅ Answer: ≈ 62.14**
