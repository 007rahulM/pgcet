# Blood Relations: Pattern 1 (Pointing to a Photo) — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** Pointing to a lady, a man says, "She is the daughter of the woman who is the mother of the husband of my mother." Who is she to the man?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A man describes a lady through a multi-step chain involving his mother's husband's mother
- Find: The lady's relationship to the man

**💡 What I'll use:** Trace the chain step by step, then draw a mini family tree

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: "The husband of my mother" = the man's father

Step 2: "The mother of the husband of my mother" = the mother of the man's father = the man's **grandmother** (paternal)

Step 3: "The daughter of my grandmother" = the man's father's sister = the man's **aunt**

```
Grandmother
     │
   ┌─┴──────────┐
 Father        She (Aunt)
     │
    Man
```

**✅ Answer: She is the man's Aunt**

### Q2

**❓ Question:** A man says, "That boy's mother is the only daughter of my mother." How is the man related to the boy?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A man says the boy's mother is the only daughter of the man's mother
- Find: How the man is related to the boy

**💡 What I'll use:** "Only daughter of my mother" = the man's sister

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: "Only daughter of my mother" = the man's sister (his mother's only daughter)

Step 2: "The boy's mother" = the man's sister

Step 3: The man is the sister's brother → he is the boy's **maternal uncle**

```
Man's Mother
     │
   ┌─┴─────────────┐
  Man         Boy's Mother (Man's Sister)
                     │
                    Boy
```

**✅ Answer: The man is the boy's Maternal Uncle**

### Q3

**❓ Question:** Pointing to a photograph, a woman says, "He is the son of my father's only son." Who is the person in the photograph to the woman?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A woman says the photographed person is the son of her father's only son
- Find: The relationship of the person in the photo to the woman

**💡 What I'll use:** "My father's only son" = the woman's brother (or herself if she's the only child — but since she's female, it means her brother)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: "My father's only son" = the woman's brother

Step 2: "Son of my brother" = the woman's nephew

```
Father
   │
 ┌─┴──────┐
Brother  Woman
   │
  He (Nephew)
```

**✅ Answer: He is the woman's Nephew**

### Q4

**❓ Question:** Introducing a lady, a man says, "She is the wife of the only son of my grandfather." Who is the lady to the man?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A man says the lady is the wife of the only son of his grandfather
- Find: The lady's relationship to the man

**💡 What I'll use:** "Only son of my grandfather" = the man's father (since grandfather's only son)

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: "Only son of my grandfather" = the man's father

Step 2: "Wife of my father" = the man's mother

```
Grandfather
     │
   Father ═══ She (Lady)
       │
      Man
```

**✅ Answer: She is the man's Mother**

### Q5

**❓ Question:** A says, "The mother of B is the only daughter of my mother." How is A related to B?

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: A says B's mother is the only daughter of A's mother
- Find: A's relationship to B

**💡 What I'll use:** "Only daughter of my mother" = A's sister

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: "Only daughter of my (A's) mother" = A's sister

Step 2: "B's mother" = A's sister

Step 3: A is the brother of B's mother → A is B's **maternal uncle**

```
A's Mother
     │
   ┌─┴─────────────┐
   A            A's Sister (B's Mother)
                        │
                        B
```

**✅ Answer: A is B's Maternal Uncle**

---

> 💡 **Method for all photo-type problems:** Parse the description from the right (starting at the photo person) step by step back to the narrator. Draw the family tree at each step — never try to solve purely in your head.
