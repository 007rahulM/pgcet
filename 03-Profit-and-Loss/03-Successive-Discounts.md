# Successive Discounts — Two or More Discounts Applied One After Another

## 🔍 How to Recognize This Pattern

Look for:
- "Two discounts of D1% and D2% given successively"
- "First 20% discount, then another 10% discount"
- "What is the net/equivalent single discount?"

---

## 🧠 The Concept (Why Not Simply Add Discounts)

**Wrong:** 20% + 10% = 30% discount ← **INCORRECT!**

**Why?** The second discount is applied on the **already reduced price**, not the original.

---

## 📐 Key Formula

### Net Single Discount (for two discounts D1 and D2):
> **Net discount = D1 + D2 − (D1 × D2) / 100**

### SP after two successive discounts:
> SP = MP × (100 − D1)/100 × (100 − D2)/100

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step Examples

### Example 1

**Problem:** MP = ₹1000. Discounts: 20% and 10%. Find SP and net discount.

**Method 1 (Step by step):**
- After 20%: SP1 = 1000 × 80/100 = ₹800
- After 10%: SP2 = 800 × 90/100 = **₹720**

**Method 2 (Formula):**
- Net discount = 20 + 10 − (20×10)/100 = 30 − 2 = **28%**
- SP = 1000 × 72/100 = **₹720** ✅

---

### Example 2

**Problem:** Two discounts of 25% and 20%. Which is better: 25% then 20%, or 20% then 25%?

**Both give the same net discount!** (Formula is symmetric)
- Net = 25 + 20 − 25×20/100 = 45 − 5 = **40%** in both cases ✅

---

### Example 3 (Three successive discounts)

**Problem:** MP = ₹2000. Discounts: 10%, 20%, 25%. Find SP.

- After 10%: 2000 × 0.9 = 1800
- After 20%: 1800 × 0.8 = 1440
- After 25%: 1440 × 0.75 = **₹1080** ✅

---

### Example 4

**Problem:** A single discount of 28% is equivalent to two successive discounts of D% each. Find D.

- Net = D + D − D²/100 = 28
- 2D − D²/100 = 28 ... (Solve: try D=15: 30−225/100=30−2.25=27.75≈28)
- Exact: D = ?, try 15.5: 31 − 240.25/100 = 31−2.4 = 28.6
- Try 15: 30−2.25 = 27.75 ... Try 16: 32−2.56 = 29.44
- **Approximately D = 15.5%** (in MCQ options will be exact numbers)

---

### Example 5 (Find MP from net discount and SP)

**Problem:** After successive discounts of 10% and 20%, SP = ₹720. Find MP.

- Net = 10 + 20 − 2 = 28%
- MP × 72/100 = 720 → MP = 720 × 100/72 = **₹1000** ✅

---

## ⚡ 60-Second Shortcut

For two successive discounts D1% and D2%:
> **Net = D1 + D2 − (D1 × D2)/100**

For SP directly:
> **SP = MP × (100−D1)/100 × (100−D2)/100**

**Quick Mental Check:** Net discount is ALWAYS less than D1 + D2.

## 📝 Practice Problems

1. MP = ₹500. Discounts 10% and 20%. Find SP and net single discount.

2. Net single discount equivalent to 15% and 10%?

3. MP = ₹800. Three discounts: 10%, 10%, 10%. Final SP?

4. After discounts of 20% and 25%, SP = ₹360. Find MP.

5. Two successive discounts of 20% each. Net discount?

6. D1 = 30%, D2 = 20%. Net discount?

7. MP = ₹1500. Discounts 25% and 8%. Find SP.

8. Net single discount equivalent to 40% and 10%?

---

## ✔️ Answers

1. Net = 10+20−2 = 28%. SP = 500×0.72 = **₹360**
2. 15+10 − 1.5 = **23.5%**
3. Each time multiply by 0.9: 800×0.9×0.9×0.9 = 800×0.729 = **₹583.2**
4. Net = 20+25−5 = 40%. MP×0.6 = 360 → MP = **₹600**
5. 20+20−4 = **36%**
6. 30+20−6 = **44%**
7. Net = 25+8−2 = 31%. SP = 1500×0.69 = **₹1035**
8. 40+10−4 = **46%**
