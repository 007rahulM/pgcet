# Profit & Loss — Complete Formula Sheet

> 🎯 **HOW TO USE:** Find your question type below. Every formula, including reverse calculations, is listed.

---

## ⚡ KEY ABBREVIATIONS
- **CP** = Cost Price (what you buy at)
- **SP** = Selling Price (what you sell at)
- **MP** = Marked Price (labelled/listed price)
- **Profit** = when SP > CP
- **Loss** = when SP < CP

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 1 — Basic Profit and Loss

### Question looks like:
- "Bought for ₹400, sold for ₹500. Profit %?"
- "Sold at **20% profit**. SP = ₹720. Find CP."
- "Sold at **10% loss**. CP = ₹600. Find SP."
- "CP = ₹800. Sold at 15% profit. Find SP."
- "SP = ₹360, loss = 10%. Find CP."

### ALL Formulas:

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Profit amount | `SP − CP` |
| Loss amount | `CP − SP` |
| Profit % | `(Profit ÷ CP) × 100` |
| Loss % | `(Loss ÷ CP) × 100` |
| SP when CP and Profit% given | `CP × (100 + Profit%) ÷ 100` |
| SP when CP and Loss% given | `CP × (100 − Loss%) ÷ 100` |
| CP when SP and Profit% given | `SP × 100 ÷ (100 + Profit%)` |
| CP when SP and Loss% given | `SP × 100 ÷ (100 − Loss%)` |
| CP when Profit amount and Profit% given | `Profit × 100 ÷ Profit%` |
| CP when Loss amount and Loss% given | `Loss × 100 ÷ Loss%` |

### ⚠️ Golden Rule:
> **Profit% and Loss% are ALWAYS calculated on CP, never on SP.**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 2 — Discount and Marked Price

### Question looks like:
- "Marked price = ₹1000. Discount = 10%. SP?"
- "SP = ₹900 after 10% discount. Find MP."
- "Discount given = ₹150 on MP ₹750. Discount %?"
- "Shopkeeper marks 20% above CP and gives 10% discount. Profit%?"

### ALL Formulas:

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Discount amount | `MP − SP` |
| Discount % | `(Discount ÷ MP) × 100` |
| SP after discount | `MP × (100 − Discount%) ÷ 100` |
| MP given SP and discount% | `SP × 100 ÷ (100 − Discount%)` |
| Profit% (MP marked above CP, discount given) | Find SP from MP, then `(SP − CP) ÷ CP × 100` |

→ **See examples: [02-Discount.md](./02-Discount.md)**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 3 — Successive Discounts

### Question looks like:
- "Two discounts of 20% and 10%. Equivalent single discount?"
- "First discount 25%, then 20%. Final SP on MP = ₹1000?"
- "Net effective discount for discounts a% and b%?"

### ALL Formulas:

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Net single discount for two successive discounts a% and b% | `a + b − (a × b) / 100` |
| SP after two successive discounts on MP | `MP × (100−a)/100 × (100−b)/100` |
| SP after three discounts a%, b%, c% | `MP × (100−a)/100 × (100−b)/100 × (100−c)/100` |

→ **See examples: [03-Successive-Discounts.md](./03-Successive-Discounts.md)**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 4 — Dishonest Dealer / False Weights

### Question looks like:
- "Shopkeeper uses **900g instead of 1000g**. Profit%?"
- "Uses false weight. Claims to sell at CP. Actual profit%?"
- "Buys using 900g weight, sells using 1100g weight. Net profit?"
- "Milkman mixes 20% water in milk. Sells at CP. Profit%?"
- "Dealer buys at ₹80/kg, sells at ₹100/kg, gives only 900g. Profit%?"

### ALL Formulas:

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Profit% (claiming to sell true weight, actually gives less) | `(True weight − False weight) ÷ False weight × 100` |
| Profit% (error in weight, sells at CP) | `Error ÷ (True value − Error) × 100` |
| Actual profit% (different buy price and sell price + false weight) | Find actual CP and SP per unit delivered, compute `(SP−CP)/CP × 100` |

→ **See examples: [04-Dishonest-Dealer.md](./04-Dishonest-Dealer.md)**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 5 — Same SP, One at Profit / One at Loss

### Question looks like:
- "Two items sold at ₹1200 each. One at **20% profit**, other at **20% loss**. Net result?"
- "Sold two watches at same price with equal profit% and loss%. Overall?"

### Key Result:
> When **same SP** and **equal profit% = loss%**, there is **always a net loss**.
> **Net Loss% = (Common %)² ÷ 100**

| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Net loss% | `(x²) ÷ 100` where x = common profit% = loss% |
| CP of profit item | `SP × 100 ÷ (100 + Profit%)` |
| CP of loss item | `SP × 100 ÷ (100 − Loss%)` |

→ **See examples: [01-Basic-Concepts.md](./01-Basic-Concepts.md)**

## 📐 FORMULA BLOCK 6 — Bulk / Count Problems

### Question looks like:
- "CP of **20 articles = SP of 25 articles**. Profit or loss%?"
- "A sold 100 apples at CP of 120 apples. Profit%?"
- "Bought 10 items for ₹100. Sold 9 items for ₹100. Profit%?"

### Approach:
| Find | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Profit% (CP of x = SP of y) | If x > y → profit, if x < y → loss; `(x−y)/y × 100` |
| Effective profit% | `(Actual revenue − Actual cost) ÷ Actual cost × 100` |

## 🔑 Master Summary Table

| Formula | Used When |
|---------|-----------|
| `SP − CP` | Finding profit amount |
| `CP − SP` | Finding loss amount |
| `(Profit/CP) × 100` | Finding profit% |
| `CP × (100+P%)/100` | Finding SP when CP and profit% given |
| `SP × 100/(100+P%)` | Finding CP when SP and profit% given |
| `MP × (100−D%)/100` | SP after one discount |
| `a+b−ab/100` | Net equivalent single discount for two discounts |
| `(True−False)/False × 100` | Profit from false weights |
| `x²/100` | Net loss% when same SP at equal profit/loss |
