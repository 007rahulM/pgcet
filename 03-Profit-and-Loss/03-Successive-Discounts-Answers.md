# Profit & Loss: Successive Discounts — Practice Problem Solutions

### Q1

**❓ Question:** MP = ₹500. Discounts 10% and 20%. Find SP and net single discount.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: MP = ₹500, two successive discounts of 10% then 20%
- Find: Final SP and the equivalent single discount

**💡 What I'll use:** SP = MP × (100−D1)/100 × (100−D2)/100; Net discount = D1 + D2 − (D1×D2)/100

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Apply both discounts sequentially
SP = 500 × 0.90 × 0.80 = 500 × 0.72 = **₹360**

Step 2: Calculate net single discount
Net = 10 + 20 − (10 × 20)/100 = 30 − 2 = **28%**

**✅ Answer: SP = ₹360, Net single discount = 28%**

### Q3

### Q4

**❓ Question:** After discounts of 20% and 25%, SP = ₹360. Find MP.

**🤔 What I understood:**
- Given: Two successive discounts of 20% and 25% yield SP = ₹360
- Find: Original Marked Price

**💡 What I'll use:** MP × (1 − 0.20) × (1 − 0.25) = SP; solve for MP

**✏️ My Solution:**

Step 1: Set up the equation
MP × 0.80 × 0.75 = 360
MP × 0.60 = 360

Step 2: Solve for MP
MP = 360 / 0.60 = **₹600**

**✅ Answer: MP = ₹600**
