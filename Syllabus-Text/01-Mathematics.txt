PGCET MCA 2025–26 — Syllabus Extract
Section: Mathematics

Algebra:
- Fundamental operations in algebra
- Expansion and factorization
- Simultaneous linear and quadratic equations
- Indices and logarithms
- Arithmetic/Geometric/Harmonic progressions
- Binomial theorem
- Permutations and combinations
- Determinants and matrices
- Sets and applications to solving simultaneous linear equations

Coordinate Geometry:
- Rectangular Cartesian coordinates
- Equations of a line, midpoint, intersections
- Equations of a circle
- Distance formulae
- Pair of straight lines
- Parabola, ellipse, hyperbola
- Geometric transformations: translation, rotation, scaling

Boolean Algebra:
- Basic operations
- Evaluation of Boolean expressions
- Basic postulates of Boolean algebra

Trigonometry:
- Simple identities
- Trigonometric equations
- Properties of triangles
- Inverse functions

Probability & Statistics:
- Basic probability theory
- Averages
- Dependent and independent events
- Frequency distributions and measures of dispersion
- Skewness and kurtosis
- Correlation and regression
- Random variables
